Baked Cod with Lemon Caper Sauce & Roasted Broccoli

Week 2 ‚Ä¢ Sunday Dinner
Cuisine: Mediterranean Style: Sheet Pan + Pan Prep: 10 min Cook: 25 min Total: 35 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs cod fillets (buy fresh Sunday!)
  • 2 large heads broccoli, cut into florets
  • 3 tbsp olive oil (divided)
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp capers, drained
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425¬∞F
  2. Prep broccoli: Toss florets with 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread on baking sheet.
  3. Roast broccoli: 20 minutes until tender and slightly charred.
  4. Season cod: Pat fillets dry. Sprinkle with paprika, salt, and pepper.
  5. Cook cod: Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in large skillet over medium-high. Add cod and cook 4 minutes per side until flaky and opaque.
  6. Make sauce: Remove cod from pan. Add butter, garlic, capers, and lemon juice to the hot pan. Swirl 1 minute until garlic is fragrant.
  7. Plate: Spoon sauce over cod. Serve with roasted broccoli.

Portion Sizes

Alexander: 8 oz cod + sauce + 2 cups broccoli

Hanna: 6 oz cod + sauce + 1.5 cups broccoli
