Sweet Potato & Turkey Breakfast Hash

Prep time: 15 mins | Cook time: 30 mins

Ingredients (10 Portions)

  • Ground Turkey: 96.5oz raw total (Zander 5x 12oz, Hanna 5x 7.3oz)
  • 4 large Sweet Potatoes (cubed)
  • 3 Red Bell Peppers (diced)
  • 4 cups Spinach (fresh)
  • 1 cup Green Onion tops (chopped)
  • 4 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Turmeric
  • 1 tbsp Cumin
  • Sea salt

Instructions

  1. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a very large skillet or pot. Add cubed sweet potatoes and cook until softened and slightly browned (approx 15-20 mins).
  2. Add another 2 tbsp oil and the ground turkey. Season with turmeric, cumin, and salt.
  3. Brown the turkey while breaking it up.
  4. Add diced bell peppers and green onion tops. Cook for 5 mins.
  5. Fold in spinach at the end until wilted.
  6. Cool and portion into 10 containers.

Portioning

  • Zander: 5 portions (approx 9oz cooked weight each)
  • Hanna: 5 portions (approx 5.5oz cooked weight each)

Medical Compliance

Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Egg-free (Breakfast safe!), Soy-free. No garlic/onion pieces.
