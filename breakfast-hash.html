Low-FODMAP Egg-Free "Power" Hash

Week 3 • Sunday Breakfast Prep
Style: Savory Meal Prep (Low-FODMAP) Prep: 15 min Cook: 30 min Total: 45 min Makes: 14 portions (7 days x 2 people)

Strategic Ingredients

  • 4 lbs Ground Turkey (Plain/unseasoned to avoid hidden garlic/onion)
  • 6-8 large Yukon Gold Potatoes (cubed - approx 4 lbs total)
  • 2 bags Spinach or Kale (wilted in at the end)
  • 3 Red Bell Peppers (diced)
  • 2 bunches Spring Onions (GREEN PARTS ONLY - discard white bulbs)
  • Seasoning: Garlic-infused olive oil, Smoked paprika, sea salt, black pepper.

Instructions

Step 1: Roast Yukon Gold Potatoes (30 min)

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Cube 6-8 large Yukon Gold potatoes (1/2-inch cubes). Leave skins on for nutrients.
  3. Toss with garlic-infused olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for 25-30 minutes until golden and tender.

Step 2: Brown Meat & Veggies (15 min)

  1. In a very large skillet or pot, heat 1-2 tbsp garlic-infused oil.
  2. Brown 4 lbs ground turkey. Break up well as it cooks.
  3. Add diced 3 red bell peppers and the green parts only of the spring onions. Cook until softened.
  4. Season generously with smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. (Avoid garlic/onion powder).

Step 3: Combine & Wilt (5 min)

  1. Add the roasted Yukon Gold potatoes to the meat mixture.
  2. Fold in 2 bags of spinach or kale. Cook for 2-3 minutes until wilted.

Step 4: Portion & Store

  1. Alexander (7 containers): 8 oz meat base + 1.5 cups potato + heavy greens.
  2. Hanna (7 containers): 5 oz meat base + 1 cup potato + moderate greens.

Target Weights (Cooked)

Person Meat Base Yukon Gold Potato
Alexander 8 oz 1.5 cups
Hanna 5 oz 1 cup

Reheating

Oven: 350°F for 10 minutes. Yukon Golds hold their texture better than sweet potatoes when reheated this way.

Low-FODMAP Tip: Top with fresh avocado (limit to 1/8th per serving to stay safe) or a dash of hot sauce (check labels for garlic/onion powder).
