Potato, Parsnip & Turkey Breakfast Hash

Prep time: 15 mins | Cook time: 30 mins

Ingredients (10 Portions)

  • Ground Turkey: 96.5oz raw total (Zander 5x 12oz, Hanna 5x 7.3oz)
  • 4 large Regular Potatoes (Yukon Gold or Russet, **Peeled** and cubed)
  • 3 large Parsnips (**Peeled** and cubed)
  • 2 cups Shredded Carrots
  • 3 Yellow Bell Peppers (diced)
  • 4 cups Spinach (fresh)
  • 1 cup Green Onion tops (chopped)
  • 4 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Turmeric
  • 1 tbsp Cumin
  • Sea salt

Instructions

  1. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a very large skillet or pot. Add peeled and cubed potatoes and parsnips. Cook until softened and slightly browned (approx 15-20 mins).
  2. Add another 2 tbsp oil, the ground turkey, and shredded carrots. Season with turmeric, cumin, and salt.
  3. Brown the turkey while breaking it up.
  4. Add diced yellow bell peppers and green onion tops. Cook for 5 mins.
  5. Fold in spinach at the end until wilted.
  6. Cool and portion into 10 containers.

Portioning

  • Zander: 5 portions (approx 9oz cooked weight each)
  • Hanna: 5 portions (approx 5.5oz cooked weight each)

Medical Compliance

Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Egg-free (Breakfast safe!), Soy-free. No garlic/onion pieces. **No sweet potatoes.**
