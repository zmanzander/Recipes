Low-FODMAP Egg-Free "Power" Hash

3-Day Prep Strategy: Sun-Wed + Wed Night (Thu-Sat)
Style: Savory Meal Prep (Low-FODMAP) Prep: 15 min Cook: 30 min Strategy: Sunday prep (Sun-Wed), Wednesday night prep (Thu-Sat)

🥘 PART 1: Sunday Prep (Sun-Wed Breakfasts)

Makes: 8 portions (4 days × 2 people)

Ingredients (Sunday Prep)

  • 5 lbs Ground Turkey (Plain/unseasoned to avoid hidden garlic/onion)
  • 4-5 large Yukon Gold Potatoes (cubed - approx 2.5 lbs total)
  • 1.5 bags Spinach or Kale (wilted in at the end)
  • 2 Red Bell Peppers (diced)
  • 1 bunch Spring Onions (GREEN PARTS ONLY - save 0.5 bunch for Wednesday)
  • Seasoning: Garlic-infused olive oil, Smoked paprika, Salt, Pepper.

Instructions (Sunday Prep)

  1. Roast Yukon Gold Potatoes (30 min): Preheat oven to 400°F. Cube 4-5 large Yukon Gold potatoes (½-inch cubes). Leave skins on for nutrients. Toss with garlic-infused olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for 25-30 minutes until golden and tender.
  2. Brown Meat & Veggies (15 min): In a very large skillet or pot, heat 1-2 tbsp garlic-infused oil. Brown 5 lbs ground turkey. Break up well as it cooks. Add diced 2 red bell peppers and the green parts only of 1 bunch spring onions. Cook until softened. Season generously with smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. (Avoid garlic/onion powder).
  3. Combine & Wilt (5 min): Add the roasted Yukon Gold potatoes to the meat mixture. Fold in 1.5 bags of spinach or kale. Cook for 2-3 minutes until wilted.
  4. Portion & Store (8 containers):
    • Alexander (4 containers): 8 oz meat base + 1.5 cups potato + heavy greens per container.
    • Hanna (4 containers): 5 oz meat base + 1 cup potato + moderate greens per container.

🔥 PART 2: Wednesday Night Prep (Thu-Sat Breakfasts)

Timing: Cook Wednesday evening after dinner. Store in fridge for Thu-Sat mornings.

Makes: 6 portions (3 days × 2 people)

Ingredients (Wednesday Night)

  • 4 lbs Ground Turkey (Plain/unseasoned)
  • 3-4 large Yukon Gold Potatoes (cubed - approx 2 lbs total)
  • 1 bag Spinach or Kale
  • 1.5 Red Bell Peppers (diced)
  • 0.5 bunch Spring Onions (GREEN PARTS ONLY)
  • Seasoning: Garlic-infused olive oil, Smoked paprika, Salt, Pepper.

Instructions (Wednesday Evening - 30 min)

  1. Roast potatoes: Preheat oven to 400°F. Cube 3-4 potatoes, toss with garlic-infused oil, roast 25-30 minutes.
  2. Brown turkey: In large skillet, brown 4 lbs ground turkey in garlic-infused oil. Add 1.5 red bell peppers and green spring onion tops. Season well.
  3. Combine: Add roasted potatoes and fold in 1 bag spinach/kale until wilted.
  4. Portion into 6 containers:
    • Alexander (3 containers): 8 oz meat + 1.5 cups potato + heavy greens.
    • Hanna (3 containers): 5 oz meat + 1 cup potato + moderate greens.
  5. Store in fridge. Reheat Thursday-Saturday mornings.

Target Weights (Cooked)

Person Meat Base Yukon Gold Potato
Alexander 8 oz 1.5 cups
Hanna 5 oz 1 cup

Why This Strategy?

Food Safety: 3-4 day max storage keeps everything fresh and safe. Thursday's breakfast is only 1 night old when eaten.

Better Quality: Thursday-Saturday breakfasts taste fresher than week-old meal prep.

Manageable Wednesday Cook: Takes 30 minutes—much less than a full Sunday prep.

Reheating

Oven: 350°F for 10 minutes. Yukon Golds hold their texture better than sweet potatoes when reheated this way.

Low-FODMAP Tip: Top with fresh avocado (limit to ⅛th per serving to stay safe) or a dash of hot sauce (check labels for garlic/onion powder).
