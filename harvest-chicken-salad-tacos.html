Harvest Chicken Salad Tacos (GF/DF)

Cuisine: American / Fusion Diet: Gluten-Free, Dairy-Free Prep: 20 min Cook: 5 min Stores: 3-4 days (Salad only)

Ingredients

Chicken Salad Base

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cooked and shredded (or rotisserie)
  • 2 celery stalks, finely diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely diced
  • 1/2 cup red grapes, halved (or dried cranberries)
  • 1/3 cup pecans or walnuts, chopped
  • 1/3 cup avocado mayo (DF)
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder to taste

Assembly & Sides

  • 4-6 Rise & Puff GF Tortillas (or Siete Almond Flour)
  • 2 cups arugula
  • 1 lemon (juiced)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 cup mixed berries (blackberries, raspberries, blueberries)
  • Fresh dill or parsley (optional garnish)

Instructions

  1. Prep Chicken Salad: In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, diced celery, onion, grapes, nuts, avocado mayo, Dijon mustard, and seasonings. Mix well until combined. Taste and adjust salt/pepper.
  2. Warm Tortillas: Heat a dry skillet over medium-high heat. Warm tortillas for 30-60 seconds per side until they puff slightly and become pliable. Keep warm in a clean towel.
  3. Dress Arugula: In a separate bowl, toss the arugula with lemon juice, olive oil, a pinch of salt, and crushed red pepper flakes.
  4. Assemble: Fill each warm tortilla with a generous scoop of chicken salad. Top with fresh dill if using.
  5. Serve: Plate 2-3 tacos per person with the dressed arugula salad and mixed berries on the side.

Portion Sizes

Zander: 3 tacos (approx. 8-9 oz chicken salad total) + 1.5 cups arugula salad + 1/2 cup berries

Hanna: 2 tacos (approx. 6 oz chicken salad total) + 1 cup arugula salad + 1/2 cup berries

Notes

Storage: Store the chicken salad in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3-4 days. Store arugula separately (undressed). Warm tortillas fresh before eating.

Meal Prep: Excellent for lunches. Pack chicken salad and tortillas separately to prevent sogginess.
