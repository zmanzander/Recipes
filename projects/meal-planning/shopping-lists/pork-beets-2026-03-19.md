# Shopping List - Pork Tenderloin with Pickled Beets
## Recipe for: Dinner March 19 + Hanna's Lunch March 21 (3 servings total)

### 🥩 Proteins
- [ ] Pork tenderloin - 1.35 lbs (raw weight for 16 oz cooked)

### 🥬 Produce  
- [ ] Mixed greens (arugula or spring mix) - 6 cups
- [ ] Shallot - 1 small

### 🥛 Dairy
- [ ] Goat cheese - 6 oz (crumbled)

### 🌾 Pantry
- [ ] Walnuts - 3/4 cup
- [ ] Balsamic vinegar - 4.5 tbsp (if low)
- [ ] Dijon mustard - 1.5 tsp (if low)
- [ ] Dried thyme - 1.5 tsp (if low)
- [ ] Olive oil - 6 tbsp total (3 for pork, 3 for dressing)

### ❄️ Already Have
- [ ] Hanna's pickled beets - 1.5 cups sliced
- [ ] Salt & pepper

## Notes
- Pork quantity accounts for 25% cooking shrinkage (raw → cooked)
- Pickled beets should already be in fridge
- Check pantry for olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Dijon, thyme before shopping
