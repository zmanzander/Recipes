## Recipes & Instructions

### 1. Saturday Guest Dinner: Grilled Lime Salmon w/ Avocado-Mango Salsa (Tonight)
- **Ingredients:** 2.2 lbs Skinless Salmon Fillets, 1.5 cups Jasmine Rice, 1 can Full-fat Coconut Milk, 1.5 cups Coconut Water, 1 large Mango, 1 Yellow Bell Pepper, 1 large Avocado, 1 bunch Cilantro, 1 bunch Green Onions (tops only), 5 Limes, Garlic-infused Olive Oil.
- **Portions (4 total):** 
  - **Alexander:** 12 oz raw → 9 oz cooked salmon, 1.5 cups coconut rice, 1/4 cup salsa.
  - **Hanna & Guests (3):** 7.3 oz raw → 5.5 oz cooked salmon, 1 cup coconut rice, 1/4 cup salsa per person.
- **Instructions:**
  1. **Marinate (30-60 mins):** Whisk 3 tbsp garlic oil, 2 tsp lime zest, 3 tbsp lime juice, salt, pepper. Coat salmon.
  2. **Cook Rice (20 mins):** Bring rice, coconut water, 1.25 cups coconut milk, 1/2 tsp salt to boil. Simmer, cover, cook 20 mins.
  3. **Prep Salsa:** Dice mango, yellow pepper, cilantro, green onion tops, avocado. Toss with lime juice, 1 tbsp garlic oil, 1 tbsp coconut water.
  4. **Grill Salmon (6-8 mins):** Grill over **Medium-High Heat** (3-4 mins per side) until opaque.
  5. **Serve:** Top salmon with 1/4 cup salsa over coconut rice.

### 2. Saturday Guest Dessert: Salted Dark Chocolate Tart (Tonight)
- **Ingredients:** 2 cups Almond Flour, 1/4 cup Cocoa Powder, 1/4 cup Coconut Oil (melted), 3 tbsp Maple Syrup, 1 lb Dark Chocolate (70%+), 1.5 cups Coconut Milk, 1 tsp Vanilla, Sea Salt.
- **Instructions:**
  1. **Crust (12 mins):** Mix almond flour, cocoa, oil, syrup, salt. Press into 9-inch pan. Bake at **350°F** for 12 mins. Cool.
  2. **Filling:** Heat 1.5 cups coconut milk until simmering. Pour over chopped chocolate. Let sit 30s. Stir smooth. Mix in vanilla, salt.
  3. **Chill (2+ hours):** Pour into crust. Refrigerate until firm. 
  4. **Serve:** Cut into 12 slices. Top with flaky sea salt.

### 3. Shared Dinner: Pork Tenderloin with Chimichurri (Wednesday - First Meal Back)
- **Ingredients:** 1.5 lb Boneless Pork Tenderloin, 1 bunch Fresh Parsley, 1 bunch Cilantro, 1 Lemon, Red Pepper Flakes, Garlic oil, 1.5 lbs Sweet Potatoes, 1 bunch Asparagus (1 lb).
- **Portions:** 
  - **Alexander:** 10 oz raw → 7.5 oz cooked pork, 1 cup roasted sweet potatoes, 6 oz asparagus.
  - **Hanna:** 6 oz raw → 4.5 oz cooked pork, 3/4 cup roasted sweet potatoes, 6 oz asparagus.
- **Instructions:** 
  1. **Prep Vegetables:** Cut 1.5 lbs sweet potatoes into 3/4-inch cubes. Trim 1 lb asparagus, cut into 2-inch pieces.
  2. **Roast Sweet Potatoes (30 mins):** Preheat oven to **425°F**. Toss cubed sweet potatoes with 1.5 tbsp garlic oil, salt, pepper. Spread on baking sheet. Roast 30 mins, flipping halfway.
  3. **Sear Pork:** While potatoes roast, sear tenderloin in 1 tbsp garlic oil over **Medium-High Heat** (2-3 mins per side) until browned.
  4. **Roast Pork (15-20 mins):** Transfer to oven at **400°F**. Roast 15-20 mins until internal temp reaches 145°F. Rest 5 mins.
  5. **Roast Asparagus (12 mins):** When pork has 12 mins left, toss asparagus with 1 tbsp garlic oil, salt, pepper. Roast at **425°F** for 12 mins.
  6. **Make Chimichurri:** Blend 1 cup parsley, 1/2 cup cilantro, 3 tbsp lemon juice, 1/4 cup garlic oil, 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes, salt.
  7. **Serve:** Slice pork, top with chimichurri. Serve with roasted sweet potatoes and asparagus.

### 4. Shared Dinner: Crispy Skin Chicken Thighs (Thursday)
- **Ingredients:** 1.5 lbs bone-in, skin-on Chicken Thighs, 1 Lemon, 2 tbsp Capers, 1/2 cup Chicken stock, 1 lb Green Beans, 1 lb Brussels Sprouts.
- **Portions:** 
  - **Alexander:** 10 oz raw → 7.5 oz cooked chicken, 6 oz green beans, 6 oz Brussels sprouts.
  - **Hanna:** 6 oz raw → 4.5 oz cooked chicken, 6 oz green beans, 6 oz Brussels sprouts.
- **Instructions:** 
  1. **Prep Vegetables:** Trim 1 lb green beans. Trim and halve 1 lb Brussels sprouts.
  2. **Roast Brussels Sprouts (25 mins):** Preheat oven to **425°F**. Toss halved Brussels sprouts with 1.5 tbsp garlic oil, salt, pepper. Spread cut-side down on baking sheet. Roast 25 mins until caramelized.
  3. **Sear Chicken:** Pat thighs dry. Season with salt and pepper. Heat 1 tbsp garlic oil in oven-safe skillet over **Medium Heat**. Place thighs skin-side down. Sear 10-12 mins until skin is golden and crispy (don't move them!).
  4. **Roast Chicken (10 mins):** Flip thighs. Transfer skillet to oven at **400°F**. Roast 10 mins until internal temp reaches 165°F.
  5. **Roast Green Beans (12 mins):** When chicken has 12 mins left, toss green beans with 1 tbsp garlic oil, salt, pepper. Roast at **425°F** for 12 mins.
  6. **Make Pan Sauce:** Remove chicken to rest. Place skillet over **Medium-High Heat**. Add 1/2 cup chicken stock, 2 tbsp lemon juice, 2 tbsp capers. Scrape browned bits. Simmer 2-3 mins until slightly reduced.
  7. **Serve:** Plate chicken, drizzle with pan sauce. Serve with roasted Brussels sprouts and green beans.
