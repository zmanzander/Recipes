# Standing Rules for Meal Planning

## Weekly Schedule Protocol

### Saturday Rule (CRITICAL)
**Saturdays are ALWAYS FREE - no planning, no reminders, no shopping list items.**

- Do NOT include Saturday lunch or dinner in meal plans
- Do NOT send meal reminders for Saturday
- Do NOT add Saturday-specific items to shopping lists
- User handles all Saturday meals independently

### Weekly Structure
- **Sunday:** Fresh dinner + meal prep for the week
- **Monday-Thursday:** Prepped lunches, fresh dinners
- **Friday:** Fresh lunch, dinner out
- **Saturday:** FREE (user handles independently)

## Daily Meal Reminder Cron
- **Skip:** Saturday (no reminders)
- **Send:** Sunday-Friday only
- **Time:** 3:00 PM daily

## Other Standing Rules
- Fish/seafood: Always Monday or Tuesday (freshness protocol)
- Meal prep limit: 4 days max
- Guest dinners: Scale portions appropriately
- Organic zone: Raised beds, potted citrus (no synthetic fertilizers)
