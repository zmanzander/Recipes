Shrimp Stir-Fry with Vegetables

Prep time: 15 mins | Cook time: 10 mins

Ingredients (3 Portions)

  • Shrimp (peeled/deveined): 26.6oz raw total (Zander 1x 12oz, Hanna 2x 7.3oz)
  • 2 Zucchini (peeled and sliced)
  • 1 head Bok Choy (leafy green parts only)
  • 1 Yellow Bell Pepper (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp Fresh Ginger (grated)
  • 1/2 cup Green Onion tops (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • 1 cup White Rice (dry)
  • Sea salt

Instructions

  1. Cook white rice according to package instructions.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
  3. Add ginger and green onion tops, stir for 30 seconds.
  4. Add shrimp and cook until pink (2-3 mins). Remove shrimp.
  5. Add yellow bell pepper and peeled zucchini. Stir-fry for 3 mins.
  6. Add bok choy leaves and shrimp back in. Toss until bok choy is wilted. Season with salt.
  7. Serve over rice.

Portioning

  • Thursday Dinner: Zander (12oz raw), Hanna (7.3oz raw)
  • Friday Lunch: Hanna (7.3oz raw)

Medical Compliance

Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Egg-free, Soy-free. No soy sauce (use salt/ginger for flavor). No garlic/onion pieces.
