Thai Coconut Curry

Week 2 ‚Ä¢ Tue/Wed Dinner
Cuisine: Thai Style: One Pot Prep: 10 min Cook: 30 min Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs pork shoulder OR chicken thighs, cubed
  • 1 can (14 oz) full-fat coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp red curry paste
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce (gluten-free)
  • 1 tbsp coconut aminos
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 cups dry jasmine rice (for serving)
  • Vegetables:
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1/2 small yellow onion, sliced (minimal)

Instructions

  1. Prep: Cut protein into 1-inch cubes. Slice vegetables.
  2. Cook rice: Rinse 2 cups jasmine rice. Add to pot with 3 cups water. Bring to boil, reduce to simmer, cover 18 minutes. Fluff and set aside.
  3. Sear protein: Heat 1 tbsp oil in large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high. Brown pork/chicken in batches, 6-8 minutes total. Remove and set aside.
  4. Make curry base: In same pot, whisk together coconut milk, red curry paste, fish sauce, coconut aminos, and brown sugar until smooth.
  5. Simmer: Add chicken broth and seared protein. Bring to gentle simmer. Cover and cook 15 minutes (pork) or 10 minutes (chicken) until tender.
  6. Add vegetables: Add bell pepper, snap peas, and onion. Simmer uncovered 5 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  7. Double batch for Wednesday: Portion half into containers for tomorrow's dinner.

Portion Sizes (per meal)

Alexander: 8 oz protein + 1.5 cups rice + generous curry sauce + vegetables

Hanna: 6 oz protein + 1 cup rice + curry sauce + vegetables
