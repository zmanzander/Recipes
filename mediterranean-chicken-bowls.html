Mediterranean Chicken Bowls

Week 2 ‚Ä¢ Sunday Meal Prep
Cuisine: Mediterranean Style: Meal Prep Prep: 20 min Cook: 70 min Total: 90 min Makes: 8 lunches total

What You're Making

  • Zander: 4 lunches (Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri)
  • Hanna: 4 lunches (Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu)

Ingredients

Proteins

  • 24 oz chicken breast ‚Äî for Zander's portions
  • 20 oz chicken breast ‚Äî for Hanna's portions

Marinade

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Vegetables

  • 2 zucchini, cut into 1-inch chunks
  • 2 red bell peppers, cut into chunks
  • 1 lb asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil (for roasting)
  • Salt and pepper

Rice

  • 2 cups dry jasmine rice (yields 6 cups cooked)
  • 4 cups water
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

Step 1: Marinate Chicken (10 min)

  1. Mix all marinade ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Slice chicken breasts into uniform pieces (for even cooking).
  3. Toss chicken in marinade. Let sit at room temperature while you prep vegetables.

Step 2: Prep Vegetables (10 min)

  1. Preheat oven to 425¬∞F.
  2. Cut zucchini and bell peppers into similar-sized chunks.
  3. Trim woody ends from asparagus.
  4. Toss all vegetables with 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread on two large sheet pans (don't overcrowd).

Step 3: Cook Rice (20 min)

  1. Rinse rice until water runs clear.
  2. Combine 2 cups rice + 4 cups water + pinch salt in pot.
  3. Bring to boil, reduce to low simmer, cover.
  4. Cook 18 minutes. Remove from heat, let stand 5 minutes.
  5. Fluff with fork, set aside to cool slightly.

Step 4: Roast Vegetables (25 min)

  1. Roast vegetables at 425¬∞F for 20-25 minutes, stirring halfway.
  2. Remove when tender and slightly caramelized.

Step 5: Cook Chicken (15 min)

  1. Heat grill pan or large skillet over medium-high.
  2. Cook chicken in batches 6-7 minutes per side until 165¬∞F internal.
  3. Let rest 5 minutes, then slice against the grain.

Step 6: Portion & Store (10 min)

  1. Zander's containers (4): 6 oz chicken + 1.5 cups rice + 1 cup vegetables
  2. Hanna's containers (4): 5 oz chicken + 1 cup rice + 1 cup vegetables
  3. Let cool completely before sealing.
  4. Refrigerate up to 5 days.

Portion Summary

Person Chicken Rice Vegetables
Zander 6 oz 1.5 cups 1 cup
Hanna 5 oz 1 cup 1 cup

Storage & Reheating

Storage: Up to 5 days refrigerated in airtight containers.

Reheat: Microwave 3-4 minutes, stirring halfway. Or oven 350¬∞F for 10 minutes.

Pro tip: Add a squeeze of fresh lemon before eating to brighten flavors!
