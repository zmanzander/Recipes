Pork Tenderloin with Pickled Beets & Goat Cheese

Week 2 ‚Ä¢ Monday Dinner
Cuisine: Mediterranean/American Style: Sheet Pan + Assembly Prep: 10 min Cook: 25 min Total: 35 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs pork tenderloin
  • Hanna's pickled beets ‚Äî 1 cup, sliced
  • 4 oz goat cheese, crumbled
  • 4 cups mixed greens (arugula or spring mix)
  • 1/2 cup walnuts, toasted
  • 1 small shallot, minced (light onion)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil (for pork)
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • Balsamic dressing: 3 tbsp balsamic vinegar, 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp Dijon mustard

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400¬∞F
  2. Season pork: Mix 2 tbsp olive oil, thyme, salt, and pepper. Rub all over tenderloin.
  3. Sear pork: Heat oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Sear tenderloin 3 minutes per side until browned.
  4. Roast: Transfer skillet to oven. Roast 15 minutes until internal temp reaches 145¬∞F.
  5. Rest: Remove from oven, tent with foil, rest 5 minutes.
  6. Make dressing: Whisk balsamic vinegar, olive oil, and Dijon together.
  7. Toast walnuts: In dry pan over medium heat, toast walnuts 3-4 minutes until fragrant.
  8. Slice pork: Cut into medallions against the grain.
  9. Assemble plates: Bed of mixed greens + sliced pork + pickled beets + crumbled goat cheese + toasted walnuts. Drizzle with balsamic dressing.

Portion Sizes

Alexander: 6 oz pork + 2 cups greens + 1/2 cup beets + 2 oz goat cheese

Hanna: 5 oz pork + 1.5 cups greens + 1/2 cup beets + 1.5 oz goat cheese
