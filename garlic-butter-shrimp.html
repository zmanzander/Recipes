Garlic Butter Shrimp with Rice

Cuisine: Mediterranean Style: Quick Prep: 10 min Cook: 15 min Stores: 3 days max

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked white rice for serving
  • 1 lb green beans, steamed or roasted

Instructions

  1. Pat shrimp dry with paper towels. Season lightly with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, melt 2 tbsp butter. Add shrimp in batches (don't overcrowd). Cook 1-2 minutes per side until pink. Remove and set aside.
  3. Reduce heat to medium. Add remaining butter and garlic. Cook 1-2 minutes until fragrant (don't burn).
  4. Return shrimp to pan. Add paprika, red pepper flakes, and lemon juice. Toss to coat.
  5. Remove from heat, stir in fresh parsley.
  6. Serve over rice with green beans and lemon wedges.

Portion Sizes

Alexander: 10 oz shrimp (12-15 pieces) + 1.5 cups rice + 1 cup green beans

Wife: 6 oz shrimp (8-10 pieces) + 1 cup rice + 1 cup green beans

Notes

Stores 3 days max (shrimp doesn't keep as long). Store shrimp separately from rice to prevent sogginess. Best eaten within 3 days. Shrimp cooks VERY fast — don't overcook or it becomes rubbery.
