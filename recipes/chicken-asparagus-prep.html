Lunch Prep: Lemon-Herb Chicken & Asparagus

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Time: 40 mins
Storage: 4 days in fridge
Prep Batch: 6 portions total

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs Chicken Breast (sliced into strips)
  • 2 bunches Asparagus (trimmed)
  • 2.5 cups Jasmine Rice (dry)
  • 4 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • 1 whole Lemon (juiced)
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Parsley (chopped)
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

Cook the 2.5 cups dry rice in a rice cooker or pot with 3.75 cups water.
While rice cooks, heat 2 tbsp garlic oil in a large skillet over Medium-High Heat.
Add the 3 lbs chicken strips. Sauté until golden and internal temp reaches 165°F. Instruction Update: Season with salt and pepper halfway through cooking once browning starts.
Remove chicken. Add remaining 2 tbsp oil and the 2 bunches asparagus. Sauté over Medium Heat for 4-5 mins until bright green and tender-crisp.
In a small bowl, whisk the lemon juice and chopped parsley together.
Portion the rice, chicken, and asparagus into containers. Drizzle the lemon-parsley mixture over the chicken while still warm.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)

Alexander: 10 oz cooked chicken + 1.5 cups rice + 1/2 bunch asparagus.

Hanna: 6 oz cooked chicken + 1 cup rice + 1/3 bunch asparagus.
