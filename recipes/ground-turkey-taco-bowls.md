# Ground Turkey Taco Bowls (Wed-Thu)
*Prep Day: Sunday*

**Ingredients:**
- 2 lbs Ground Turkey (93/7)
- 1 cup Jasmine Rice (Dry)
- 2 large Zucchini (shredded)
- 4 large Carrots (shredded)
- **Taco Seasoning:**
  - 1 tbsp Chili powder
  - 1 tbsp Cumin
  - 1 tsp Garlic powder
  - 1 tsp Onion powder
  - 1 tsp Salt
  - 1/2 tsp Paprika

**Instructions:**
1. Cook Jasmine rice (1 cup dry) according to package instructions.
2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown ground turkey (with 1 tbsp olive oil) until cooked through.
3. Drain excess fat if necessary.
4. Add all taco seasoning spices and 1/4 cup water. Simmer for 5-7 minutes until thickened.
5. Shred zucchini and carrots in a food processor or with a grater.
6. Portion into containers.

**Zander Portion:** 8 oz turkey, 1.5 cups rice, shredded veggies.
**Hanna Portion:** 5 oz turkey, 1 cup rice, shredded veggies.

**Storage:** Store in airtight containers for 3-4 days. Reheat in microwave (2-3 min) or pan.
