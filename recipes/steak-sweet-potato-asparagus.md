# Pan-Seared Steak with Roasted Sweet Potato & Asparagus (Sat-Sun)
*Prep Day: Saturday or Sunday (Weekend)*

**Ingredients:**
- 2 lbs Steak (Sirloin or Ribeye)
- 4 Sweet Potatoes
- 2 bunches Asparagus
- **Seasoning:**
  - 2 tbsp Olive oil
  - 1 tsp Sea salt
  - 1 tsp Black pepper
  - 1 tbsp Rosemary (fresh or dried)

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Cut sweet potatoes into 1-inch cubes. Toss with 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper.
3. Roast sweet potatoes on a baking sheet for 25-30 minutes until tender and golden.
4. Toss asparagus with 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper.
5. Roast asparagus on a separate baking sheet for 10-12 minutes until tender.
6. Season steak with salt, pepper, and rosemary.
7. Heat a large skillet over high heat with 1 tbsp olive oil.
8. Sear steak for 3-5 minutes per side (for medium-rare) until internal temperature is 135°F (57°C).
9. Let steak rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing.

**Zander Portion:** 10 oz steak, 1.5 cups sweet potato, 1 bunch asparagus.
**Hanna Portion:** 6 oz steak, 1 cup sweet potato, 1/2 bunch asparagus.

**Storage:** Best served fresh, but can be refrigerated for up to 3 days. Reheat gently in a pan.
