Shrimp Stir-Fry with Vegetables

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
⏱️ Prep: 15 mins | 🔥 Cook: 10 mins | 📦 Makes: 2 Portions

Ingredients

  • Shrimp: 20 oz raw total, peeled and deveined (Zander 1x 12oz, Hanna 1x 7.3oz)
  • Zucchini: 1 medium Zucchini, peeled and sliced into 1/2-inch half-moons
  • Bok Choy: 1 head Bok Choy (use green leafy parts only), chopped
  • Yellow Bell Pepper: 1 Yellow Bell Pepper, cut into 1/2-inch strips
  • Rice: 0.75 cups White Rice (dry)
  • Aromatics: 1 tsp Fresh Ginger (grated) and 1/4 cup Green Onion tops (green part only), chopped
  • Oil: 2 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • Seasoning: Sea Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook Rice: Prepare the 0.75 cups dry white rice according to package instructions.
  2. Sauté Aromatics: Heat 2 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat. Add the 1 tsp grated fresh ginger and 1/4 cup chopped green onion tops. Stir constantly for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Sear Shrimp: Add the 20 oz raw shrimp to the pan. Cook over high heat for 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently until the shrimp are pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the pan and set aside.
  4. Stir-Fry Veggies: To the same hot pan, add the 1 sliced yellow bell pepper and 1 sliced peeled zucchini. Stir-fry over high heat for 3 minutes until tender-crisp.
  5. Combine: Add the chopped green bok choy leaves and the cooked shrimp back into the pan. Toss everything together for 1 minute until the bok choy is wilted. Season with sea salt to taste.
  6. Serve: Serve immediately over the approx. 1.5 cups cooked rice.

Portioning (Cooked Weight)

  • Zander Thursday Dinner: Approx. 9 oz cooked shrimp + 1.5 cups cooked rice + half of the stir-fried vegetables.
  • Hanna Thursday Dinner: Approx. 5.5 oz cooked shrimp + 1 cup cooked rice + half of the stir-fried vegetables.

Medical Compliance

✅ Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Egg-free, Soy-free.
🚫 No soy sauce (salt/ginger/green onion for flavor).
🚫 No garlic/onion pieces (infused oil and tops only).
