🐷 Pork Tenderloin with Fresh Chimichurri

Time: 35 mins
Storage: Best fresh; 3 days in fridge
Portions: 2 (Zander & Hanna)

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs Boneless Pork Tenderloin
  • 3 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Parsley (finely chopped)
  • 1/4 cup Fresh Cilantro (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp Red pepper flakes

Equipment

  • Oven-safe skillet (cast iron preferred)
  • Small mixing bowl (for chimichurri)
  • Meat thermometer

Step-by-Step Instructions

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Pat the 1.5 lbs pork tenderloin dry with paper towels.
Heat 2 tbsp garlic-infused oil in the oven-safe skillet over Medium-High Heat.
Place the pork in the skillet. Sear for 2-3 mins per side until golden brown on all surfaces.
Instruction Update: Once browned, season all sides with 1/2 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp black pepper.
Transfer the skillet to the oven. Roast at 400°F for 15-20 mins, or until the internal temperature reaches 145°F (63°C).
While pork roasts, whisk 1 tbsp garlic-infused oil, 1/2 cup parsley, 1/4 cup cilantro, 1 tbsp lemon juice, and 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes in a small bowl to make the Chimichurri.
Remove pork from oven and let rest on a cutting board for 5-10 mins. Slice into 1/2-inch thick medallions.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)

Alexander: 9-10 oz cooked pork (approx. 2/3 of the medallions). Drizzle with 2/3 of the Chimichurri.

Hanna: 5.5-6 oz cooked pork (approx. 1/3 of the medallions). Drizzle with remaining Chimichurri.
