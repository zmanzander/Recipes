Pork Tenderloin with Fresh Chimichurri

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Time: 45 mins
Storage: 3 days in fridge (Dinner + Lunch)
Portions: 4 total (Zander & Hanna x 2 meals)

Ingredients (Shared Batch)

  • 3 lbs Boneless Pork Tenderloin
  • 4 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil (divided)
  • 1 cup Fresh Parsley (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Cilantro (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Red pepper flakes

Step-by-Step Instructions

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Pat the 3 lbs pork tenderloin dry with paper towels.
Heat 2 tbsp garlic-infused oil in a large oven-safe skillet over Medium-High Heat.
Sear the pork for 3-4 mins per side until golden brown. Work in batches if needed.
Season with 1 tsp salt and 1/2 tsp black pepper. Transfer skillet to oven.
Roast for 15-20 mins until internal temperature is 145°F (63°C).
Whisk remaining 2 tbsp oil, chopped herbs, lemon juice, and red pepper flakes in a small bowl to make the Chimichurri.
Remove pork from oven, let rest for 10 mins, then slice into medallions.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)

Alexander (2 meals): approx. 9-10 oz cooked pork per meal (approx. 2/3 of a 1.5lb raw equivalent).

Hanna (2 meals): approx. 5.5-6 oz cooked pork per meal (approx. 1/3 of a 1.5lb raw equivalent).
