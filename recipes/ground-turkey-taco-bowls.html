Ground Turkey Taco Bowls

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-Free
Week 3 • Sunday Meal Prep
Cuisine: Mexican-Inspired Style: Balanced Meal Prep Prep: 10 min Cook: 20 min Total: 30 min Makes: 5 portions total

What You're Making

  • Wednesday: Lunch only (Zander + Hanna)
  • Thursday: Zander Dinner only, Hanna Lunch + Dinner

Ingredients

Proteins

  • 3-3.5 lbs Ground Turkey (93/7)

Vegetables (Volume added to meat)

  • 2 large Zucchini, shredded
  • 3 large Carrots, shredded
  • 2 large Parsnips, shredded

Seasoning

  • 1 tbsp Chili powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1 tsp Garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt

Sides

  • 0.5 cup dry Jasmine Rice (yields ~1.5 cups cooked)

Instructions

Step 1: Shred Vegetables (5 min)

  1. Shred 2 zucchini, 3 carrots, and 2 parsnips using a box grater or food processor.

Step 2: Cook Rice (20 min)

  1. Rinse 0.5 cup jasmine rice. Combine with 0.75 cup water.
  2. Cook until tender (~15-18 min).

Step 3: Brown Turkey & Veggies (15 min)

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add 3-3.5 lbs ground turkey. Cook until mostly browned, breaking up with a spoon.
  3. Add the shredded zucchini and carrots. Cook for 5 minutes until veggies are softened and turkey is fully cooked.
  4. Stir in 1 tbsp chili powder, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp garlic powder, and 1/2 tsp salt. Mix well.

Step 4: Portion & Store (5 min)

  1. Zander's containers (2): 8 oz turkey-veg mix + 0.75 cup rice.
  2. Hanna's containers (3): 5 oz turkey-veg mix + 0.3 cup rice (or omit if low carb).

Portion Summary (Cooked)

Person Turkey/Veg Mix Rice
Alexander 8 oz 0.75 cup
Hanna 5 oz 0.3 cup

Storage & Reheating

Storage: Refrigerate for up to 4 days.

Reheat: Oven 350°F for 10 minutes. Top with fresh salsa or avocado if available!
