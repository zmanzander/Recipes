Pork Chops with Oyster Mushrooms

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
⏱️ Prep: 15 mins | 🔥 Cook: 25 mins | 📦 Makes: 3 Portions

Ingredients

  • Pork Chops: 26.6 oz raw total (Zander 1x 12oz, Hanna 2x 7.3oz)
  • Mushrooms: 8 oz Oyster Mushrooms, sliced into 1/2-inch pieces
  • Potatoes: 2 medium Regular Potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • Herbs: 2 tbsp Fresh Rosemary & Thyme, finely chopped
  • Oil: 3 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil (divided)
  • Seasoning: Sea Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Roast Potatoes: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the 2 medium cubed potatoes with 1 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil and salt. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 25 minutes until golden and tender.
  2. Season Pork: Season the 26.6 oz of pork chops with sea salt and the 2 tbsp chopped fresh rosemary and thyme.
  3. Sear Pork: Heat 1 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the pork chops and sear for 5-7 minutes per side, or until the internal temperature reaches 145°F (63°C). Remove the pork chops from the pan and let them rest for 5 minutes.
  4. Sauté Mushrooms: In the same skillet, add the remaining 1 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil and the 8 oz sliced oyster mushrooms. Sauté over medium heat for 5-7 minutes until the mushrooms are golden brown and slightly crisp at the edges.
  5. Serve: Top the rested pork chops with the sautéed mushrooms and serve with the roasted potatoes on the side.

Portioning (Cooked Weight)

  • Zander Wednesday Dinner: Approx. 9 oz cooked pork chop + all the mushrooms + 1.5 cups roasted potatoes.
  • Hanna Wednesday Dinner & Thursday Lunch: Approx. 5.5 oz cooked pork chop + 1 cup roasted potatoes per meal.

Medical Compliance

✅ Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Egg-free, Soy-free.
🚫 No garlic/onion pieces (infused oil only).
✅ Safe mushrooms (Oyster) and potatoes (No sweet potato).
