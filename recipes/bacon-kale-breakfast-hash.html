Bacon, Turkey & Kale Breakfast Hash

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Protein: Breakfast (Bacon & Turkey)Style: One PanTime: Prep: 15 min | Cook: 35 min

🛡️ Medical Protocol Adaptations (Low-FODMAP)

  • Sweet Potato: Substituted with peeled Yukon Gold potatoes per protocol.
  • Mushrooms: Swapped button mushrooms for Oyster Mushrooms (Low-FODMAP safe).
  • Aromatics: Used green onion tops (green parts only) and chives.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Turkey (93% lean)
  • 8-10 slices Bacon (nitrate-free, chopped)
  • 2 lbs Yukon Gold Potatoes (peeled and diced into 1/4 inch cubes)
  • 8 oz Oyster Mushrooms (sliced into bite-sized pieces)
  • 1 cup Shredded Carrots
  • 3 cups Kale (chopped, stems removed)
  • 1/4 cup Green Onion tops or Chives (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Parsley (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Cilantro (chopped)
  • 1 tsp Sea Salt (plus more to taste)

Instructions

  1. Crisp Bacon: Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add chopped bacon and cook until crispy. Remove bacon with a slotted spoon, leaving the fat in the pan.
  2. Brown Turkey: In the same pan, add the ground turkey. Break it apart and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove with a slotted spoon and set aside with the bacon.
  3. Brown Potatoes: Add diced potatoes to the skillet in an even layer (add a splash of garlic oil if pan is too dry). Cook for 10-12 minutes, stirring infrequently.
  4. Sauté Mushrooms & Carrots: Add the oyster mushrooms and shredded carrots. Sauté for 5-6 minutes until the mushrooms are golden and potatoes are tender.
  5. Combine: Return the crispy bacon and cooked turkey to the pan. Add the green onion tops, parsley, cilantro, and salt. Stir to combine.
  6. Wilt Kale: Add kale in batches, stirring to wilt and combine.
  7. Serve: Taste and adjust for salt. Serve warm.

Portioning Guide

Alexander: 2 generous cups of hash.

Hanna: 1.25 cups of hash.
