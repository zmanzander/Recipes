Bacon, Turkey & Kale Breakfast Hash

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Time: 50 mins
Style: One Pan

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Turkey (93% lean)
  • 8-10 slices Bacon (nitrate-free, chopped)
  • 2 lbs Yukon Gold Potatoes (peeled and diced into 1/4 inch cubes)
  • 8 oz Oyster Mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1 cup Shredded Carrots
  • 3 cups Kale (chopped, stems removed)
  • 1/4 cup Green Onion tops or Chives (sliced)
  • Fresh Parsley and Cilantro (chopped)
  • Sea Salt to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

Heat a large skillet over Medium Heat. Add chopped bacon and cook until crispy. Remove with a slotted spoon.
In the same pan, add the ground turkey. Brown and cook through, then set aside with the bacon.
Add diced potatoes in an even layer. Cook for 10-12 mins until caramelized.
Add the oyster mushrooms and shredded carrots. Sauté for 5-6 mins.
Return bacon and turkey to the pan. Add green onion tops, herbs, and salt.
Wilt the kale in batches and serve warm.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)

Alexander: 2 generous cups. Hanna: 1.25 cups.
