Bacon, Turkey & Kale Breakfast Hash

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Time: 60 mins
Style: One Pan (Large Batch)

Ingredients (Shared Batch: 10 portions)

  • 6 lbs Ground Turkey (93% lean)
  • 2 lbs Bacon (nitrate-free, chopped)
  • 4 lbs Yukon Gold Potatoes (peeled and diced into 1/4 inch cubes)
  • 24 oz Oyster Mushrooms (sliced)
  • 2 cups Shredded Carrots
  • 6 cups Kale (chopped, stems removed)
  • 1/2 cup Green Onion tops or Chives (sliced)
  • Fresh Parsley and Cilantro (chopped)
  • Sea Salt to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

Heat a large skillet or wok over Medium Heat. Add the 2 lbs chopped bacon and cook until crispy. Remove with a slotted spoon, leaving the rendered fat.
In the same pan (work in batches if needed), add the 6 lbs ground turkey. Brown and cook through, breaking up into small crumbles. Remove and set aside with the bacon.
Add the 4 lbs diced potatoes in an even layer (use two pans if necessary). Cook for 12-15 mins, tossing occasionally, until caramelized and tender.
Add the 24 oz oyster mushrooms and 2 cups shredded carrots. Sauté for 6-8 mins until mushrooms are browned and tender.
Return the bacon and turkey to the pan. Add 1/2 cup green onion tops, fresh herbs, and salt. Toss well to combine.
Wilt the 6 cups chopped kale into the mixture in batches.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)

Alexander (5 mornings): approx. 9 oz cooked turkey/bacon mix per morning.

Hanna (5 mornings): approx. 5.5 oz cooked turkey/bacon mix per morning.
