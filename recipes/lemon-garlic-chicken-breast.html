Zesty Lemon-Garlic Chicken Breast

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Week 6 • Thursday Fresh Cook
Cuisine: Mediterranean-Inspired Style: Quick Fresh Cook Prep: 10 min Cook: 20 min Total: 30 min

Ingredients

  • Protein: 1.5 lbs Chicken Breast (boneless, skinless, pounded thin)
  • Marinade/Sauce: 3 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil, 2 tbsp Lemon Juice, 1 tbsp GF/SF Tamari, 1 tsp dried Oregano, 1/2 tsp Salt, 1/4 tsp Black Pepper
  • Side: 1 lb Green Beans (trimmed)

Instructions

  1. Prep Chicken: Pound 1.5 lbs chicken breast to an even 1/2-inch thickness. This ensures juicy, quick cooking.
  2. Whisk Marinade: Mix 3 tbsp garlic oil, 2 tbsp lemon juice, 1 tbsp tamari, 1 tsp oregano, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
  3. Sear Chicken: Heat 1 tbsp garlic oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Add chicken in batches. Cook for 5-6 mins per side until golden brown and cooked through (165°F). Remove from pan and let rest.
  4. Sauté Green Beans: In the same pan, add 1 lb trimmed green beans. Toss with 1/4 cup water, cover, and steam for 3 mins. Remove lid and sauté for 2-3 mins until tender-crisp.
  5. Finish: Pour the remaining marinade into the pan over the beans. Simmer for 1 min until it forms a light glaze. Pour over the chicken and beans before serving.

Portion Guide (Cooked)

Alexander: 9 oz chicken + 1/2 the green beans.

Hanna: 6 oz chicken + 1/2 the green beans.

Medical Compliance

✅ Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Soy-free, Egg-free.
🚫 No onion/garlic pieces (infused oil only).
🟨 Clean protein focused.
