Harvest Chicken Salad Tacos (GF/DF)

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-Free
Cuisine: American / Fusion Diet: Gluten-Free, Dairy-Free Prep: 15 min Cook: 20 min Stores: 3-4 days (Salad only)

Ingredients

Chicken Prep

  • 1.25 lbs (20 oz) chicken breast, raw
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (for cooking)
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder to taste

Chicken Salad Base

  • Cooked and shredded chicken (from above)
  • 2 celery stalks, finely diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely diced
  • 1/2 cup red grapes, halved (or dried cranberries)
  • 1/3 cup pecans or walnuts, chopped
  • 1/3 cup avocado mayo (DF)
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard

Assembly & Sides

  • 4-6 Rise & Puff GF Tortillas (or Siete Almond Flour)
  • 2 cups arugula
  • 1 lemon (juiced)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 cup mixed berries (blackberries, raspberries, blueberries)
  • Fresh dill or parsley (optional garnish)

Instructions (Quantities Inline)

  1. Cook Chicken: Slice 1.25 lbs (20 oz) raw chicken breast into thinner cutlets. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook 6-7 minutes per side until 165°F. Let rest 5 minutes, then shred with two forks.
  2. Prep Chicken Salad: In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken with 2 diced celery stalks, 1/4 cup finely diced red onion, 1/2 cup halved grapes (or cranberries), 1/3 cup chopped pecans/walnuts, 1/3 cup avocado mayo, and 1 tsp Dijon mustard. Mix well. Taste and adjust salt/pepper.
  3. Warm Tortillas: Heat a dry skillet over medium-high heat. Warm 4-6 tortillas for 30-60 seconds per side until they puff slightly and become pliable. Keep warm in a clean towel.
  4. Dress Arugula: In a separate bowl, toss 2 cups arugula with 1 juiced lemon, 1 tbsp olive oil, a pinch of salt, and crushed red pepper flakes.
  5. Assemble: Fill each warm tortilla with a generous scoop of chicken salad. Top with fresh dill if using.
  6. Serve: Plate 2-3 tacos per person with the dressed arugula salad and 1 cup mixed berries on the side.

Portion Sizes

Zander: 3 tacos (approx. 8-9 oz chicken salad total) + 1.5 cups arugula salad + 1/2 cup berries

Hanna: 2 tacos (approx. 6 oz chicken salad total) + 1 cup arugula salad + 1/2 cup berries

Notes

Storage: Store the chicken salad in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3-4 days. Store arugula separately (undressed). Warm tortillas fresh before eating.

Meal Prep: Excellent for lunches. Pack chicken salad and tortillas separately to prevent sogginess.
