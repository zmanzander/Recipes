Lemon Herb Chicken Thighs with Roasted Vegetables

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-Free
Cuisine: Mediterranean Style: Sheet Pan Prep: 15 min Cook: 35 min Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs boneless chicken thighs (4-6 thighs)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 4 zucchini, cut into 1-inch chunks
  • 4 bell peppers (mixed colors), cut into strips
  • 2 large onions, cut into wedges
  • 3 tbsp olive oil (for vegetables)
  • 1 tsp salt (for vegetables)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper (for vegetables)
  • Cooked white rice for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C)
  2. Mix 3 tbsp olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Add chicken thighs and toss to coat.
  3. In another bowl, toss zucchini, bell peppers, and onions with 3 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread vegetables on two large sheet pans in a single layer. Nestle chicken thighs among the vegetables.
  5. Roast for 30-35 minutes until chicken reaches 165°F and vegetables are tender.
  6. Let cool 5 minutes before portioning.

Portion Sizes

Alexander: 8 oz chicken (2 thighs) + 1.5 cups vegetables + 1.5 cups rice

Wife: 5 oz chicken (1-2 thighs) + 1 cup vegetables + 1 cup rice

Notes

Stores 3-4 days. Best reheated in oven at 350°F for 10 min or microwave 3 min. Can substitute chicken breast (reduce cook time to 25 min).
