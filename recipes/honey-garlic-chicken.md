# Honey Garlic Chicken (Mon-Tue)
*Prep Day: Sunday*

**Ingredients:**
- 3 lbs Chicken Breast (trimmed, cubed)
- 3 cups Jasmine Rice (Dry)
- 1 bag Green Beans (steamed/sautéed)
- **Sauce:**
  - 1/4 cup Honey
  - 1/4 cup Tamari (GF)
  - 2 tbsp Minced Garlic
  - 1 tbsp Fresh Ginger (grated)
  - 1 tsp Sesame Oil

**Instructions:**
1. Cook Jasmine rice (3 cups dry) in Instant Pot or stovetop.
2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook cubed chicken (with 1 tbsp olive oil) until browned.
3. In a small bowl, whisk honey, tamari, garlic, ginger, and sesame oil.
4. Pour sauce over chicken and simmer for 5-7 minutes until thickened and chicken is cooked through.
5. Steam or sauté green beans until bright green and tender-crisp.
6. Portion into containers.

**Zander Portion:** 9 oz chicken, 1.5 cups rice, 1 cup beans.
**Hanna Portion:** 5 oz chicken, 1 cup rice, 1 cup beans.

**Storage:** Store in airtight containers for 3-4 days. Reheat in microwave (2-3 min) or pan.
