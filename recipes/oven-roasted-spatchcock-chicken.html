Oven Roasted Spatchcock Chicken with Herbs

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Time: 90 mins
Style: Oven Roasted

Ingredients (Sun Dinner + Mon Lunch)

  • 2 whole Chickens (3-4 lbs each, spatchcocked)
  • 4 whole Lemons
  • 1/2 cup Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • Fresh Rosemary, Thyme, and Parsley
  • Sea Salt

Step-by-Step Instructions

Position chickens breast side down and cut out backbones. Flip and flatten (spatchcock).
Rub both chickens with 1/2 cup infused oil and salt. Squeeze 4 lemons over the skin.
Arrange chickens in a large roasting pan (or two) with lemon slices and fresh herb sprigs tucked around them.
Roast at 400°F for 60-65 mins until internal temp is 165°F.
Serve with roasted lemons and pan drippings. Let rest for 10 mins before carving.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)

Alexander (2 meals): 1/2 chicken per meal. Hanna (2 meals): 1/4 chicken per meal.
