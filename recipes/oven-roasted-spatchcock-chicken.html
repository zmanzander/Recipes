Oven Roasted Spatchcock Chicken with Herbs

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Time: 90 mins
Style: Oven Roasted

Ingredients (Sun Dinner + Mon Lunch)

  • 1 whole Chicken (4-5 lbs, spatchcocked)
  • 2 whole Lemons
  • 1/4 cup Garlic-infused Olive Oil (divided)
  • Fresh Rosemary, Thyme, and Parsley
  • Sea Salt
  • 2 lbs Green Beans (trimmed)
  • 2 lbs Carrots (peeled and cut into 2-inch pieces)

Step-by-Step Instructions

Position chicken breast side down and cut out backbone. Flip and flatten (spatchcock).
Rub chicken with 3 tbsp infused oil and salt. Squeeze 2 lemons over the skin.
Arrange chicken in a roasting pan with lemon slices and fresh herb sprigs tucked around it.
Toss 2 lbs green beans and 2 lbs carrots with 2 tbsp garlic-infused oil, salt, and pepper. Spread on a separate baking sheet.
Roast chicken at 400°F for 60-65 mins until internal temp is 165°F. Place vegetables in oven at the same time and roast for 30-35 mins, tossing halfway through.
Serve chicken with roasted vegetables, roasted lemons, and pan drippings. Let rest for 10 mins before carving.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)

Sunday Dinner: Alexander 8 oz chicken + 6 oz vegetables, Hanna 6 oz chicken + 6 oz vegetables.

Monday Lunch: Alexander 8 oz chicken + 6 oz vegetables, Hanna 6 oz chicken + 6 oz vegetables.

(Total: ~28 oz cooked from 4-5 lb raw chicken; ~48 oz roasted vegetables from 4 lbs raw)
