Lemon Herb Boneless Chicken Thighs

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Protein: Chicken (Boneless Thighs)Style: Pan SearedTime: Prep: 10 min | Cook: 15 min

Why This Works

Boneless thighs cook quickly and stay incredibly juicy. Using garlic-infused oil and fresh herbs keeps this perfectly within the Strict Low-FODMAP protocol while delivering big Mediterranean flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs Boneless, Skinless Chicken Thighs (approx. 6-8 thighs)
  • 3 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • Juice of 1 large Lemon
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1 tsp Dried Thyme
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 lb Green Beans (trimmed)

Instructions

  1. Season: In a medium bowl, toss the boneless thighs with 2 tbsp of the garlic-infused oil, lemon juice, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Sear: Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken thighs and sear for 5-7 minutes per side until golden brown and internal temperature reaches 165°F. Remove and let rest for 5 minutes.
  3. Veggies: In the same skillet, add the remaining 1 tbsp oil and the green beans. Sauté for 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp. Season with a squeeze of lemon and a pinch of salt.
  4. Serve: Serve the thighs with the green beans on the side.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)

Alexander: 10 oz cooked chicken (approx. 4 thighs) + half the green beans.

Hanna: 6 oz cooked chicken (approx. 2-3 thighs) + half the green beans.
