Lemon Herb Boneless Chicken Thighs

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Protein: Chicken (Boneless Thighs)Style: Pan Seared (Thu Dinner + Fri Lunch)Time: Prep: 15 min | Cook: 20 min

Why This Works

Boneless thighs cook quickly and stay juicy. Using garlic-infused oil and fresh herbs keeps this within protocol while delivering Mediterranean flavor.

Ingredients (Shared Batch: 4 portions)

  • 3 lbs Boneless, Skinless Chicken Thighs (approx. 12-16 thighs)
  • 4 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil (divided)
  • Juice of 2 large Lemons
  • 2 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 2 tsp Dried Thyme
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 2 lbs Green Beans (trimmed)

Instructions

  1. Season: In a medium bowl, toss the 3 lbs boneless thighs with 3 tbsp of the garlic-infused oil, lemon juice, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Sear: Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken thighs in batches and sear for 6-8 minutes per side until golden brown and internal temperature reaches 165°F. Remove and let rest for 5 minutes.
  3. Veggies: In the same skillet, add the remaining 1 tbsp oil and the 2 lbs green beans. Sauté for 7-9 minutes until tender-crisp. Season with a squeeze of lemon and a pinch of salt.
  4. Serve: Serve the thighs with the green beans on the side. Save half for Friday lunch.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)

Alexander (2 meals): 10 oz cooked chicken (approx. 4-5 thighs) + half the green beans per meal.

Hanna (2 meals): 6 oz cooked chicken (approx. 3 thighs) + half the green beans per meal.
