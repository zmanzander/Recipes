Honey Garlic Chicken

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-Free
Week 3 • Sunday Meal Prep
Cuisine: Asian-Inspired Style: Meal Prep (Mon-Tue) Prep: 15 min Cook: 25 min Total: 40 min Makes: 8 portions total

What You're Making

  • Zander: 4 portions (Mon/Tue Lunch + Dinner)
  • Hanna: 4 portions (Mon/Tue Lunch + Dinner)

Ingredients

Proteins

  • 3 lbs Chicken Breast — trimmed and cubed into 1-inch pieces

Honey Garlic Sauce

  • 1/2 cup Honey
  • 1/3 cup Tamari (Gluten-Free soy sauce)
  • 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp Ginger, minced
  • 1 tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 1 tsp Red pepper flakes (optional)

Sides

  • 3 cups dry Jasmine Rice
  • 1 bag Green Beans (steamed or sautéed)

Instructions (Quantities Inline)

Step 1: Prep Chicken (10 min)

  1. Trim and cut 3 lbs chicken breast into 1-inch cubes.
  2. Pat dry with paper towels (important for a good sear).

Step 2: Make Sauce (5 min)

  1. Whisk together 1/2 cup honey, 1/3 cup tamari, 4 minced garlic cloves, 1 tbsp minced ginger, 1 tbsp sesame oil, and 1 tsp red pepper flakes.

Step 3: Cook Rice (20 min)

  1. Rinse 3 cups dry jasmine rice 2-3 times.
  2. Combine with 4.5 cups water and a pinch of salt.
  3. Cook in rice cooker or stovetop (bring to boil, simmer 18 min, rest 5 min).

Step 4: Sear Chicken (15 min)

  1. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat with a splash of high-heat oil (avocado or vegetable).
  2. Add chicken in two batches to avoid overcrowding. Cook for 5-6 minutes until golden and cooked through.
  3. Pour in the honey garlic sauce and toss to coat. Simmer for 2-3 minutes until sauce thickens and glazes the chicken.

Step 5: Prepare Green Beans (10 min)

  1. Steam or sauté 1 bag green beans until tender-crisp. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 6: Portion & Store (10 min)

  1. Zander's containers (4): 9 oz cooked chicken + 1.5 cups rice + 1 cup green beans.
  2. Hanna's containers (4): 5 oz cooked chicken + 1 cup rice + 1 cup green beans.

Portion Summary (Cooked)

Storage & Reheating

Storage: Up to 5 days refrigerated.

Reheat: Oven at 350°F for 10-12 minutes (no microwave!).

🔥 Fresh Cook Variation (2 Portions)

Quick Weeknight Fresh Cook
Prep: 5 min Cook: 25 min Total: 30 min Makes: 1 meal (2 people)

Ingredients (Fresh Cook - 2 Portions)

Proteins

  • 1.2 lbs Chicken Breast — trimmed and cubed into 1-inch pieces

Honey Garlic Sauce

  • 2 tbsp Honey
  • 2.5 tbsp Tamari (Gluten-Free soy sauce)
  • 1 clove Garlic, minced
  • 3/4 tsp Ginger, minced
  • 3/4 tsp Sesame Oil
  • Pinch Red pepper flakes (optional)

Sides

  • 1 cup dry Jasmine Rice
  • 10-12 oz Green Beans (fresh or frozen, about 2 cups prepared)

Instructions (Fresh Cook)

Step 1: Start Rice (20 min)

  1. Rinse 1 cup dry jasmine rice 2-3 times.
  2. Combine with 1.5 cups water and a pinch of salt.
  3. Bring to boil → simmer 18 min → rest 5 min.

Step 2: Prep Chicken & Sauce (5 min)

  1. Cube 1.2 lbs chicken breast into 1-inch pieces, pat dry.
  2. Whisk sauce: 2 tbsp honey, 2.5 tbsp tamari, 1 minced garlic clove, 3/4 tsp ginger, 3/4 tsp sesame oil.

Step 3: Sear Chicken (8 min)

  1. Heat skillet over medium-high with splash of oil.
  2. Sear chicken 5-6 min until golden and cooked through.
  3. Add sauce, simmer 2-3 min until it glazes.

Step 4: Steam Green Beans (Final 5 min)

  1. While chicken glazes, steam or sauté 10-12 oz green beans until tender-crisp.

Step 5: Portion & Serve

  • Zander: 9 oz chicken + 1.5 cups rice + 1 cup beans
  • Hanna: 5 oz chicken + 1 cup rice + 1 cup beans

Portion Verification
