# Salted Dark Chocolate Tart

  - ✅ Safe substitutes: Maple syrup used (instead of honey)

## This Week's Prep: Special Occasion / Dinner Party

**Making:** 1 Whole 9-inch Tart (12 servings)

### Scaled Ingredients (For 1 Tart)

**Crust (RAW):**
- **Almond Flour:** 2 cups
- **Cocoa Powder:** 1/4 cup
- **Coconut Oil (Melted):** 1/4 cup
- **Maple Syrup:** 3 tbsp
- **Sea Salt:** 1/2 tsp

**Filling:**
- **Coconut Milk (Full-fat):** 1.5 cups
- **Dark Chocolate (70%):** 1 lb (16 oz)
- **Vanilla Extract:** 1 tsp
- **Sea Salt:** 1/4 tsp

**Topping:**
- **Maldon Flaky Salt:** To taste

---

## Step-by-Step Instructions (For 1 Tart)

### Prep: 15 minutes

**1. Prepare the Crust**
- Preheat oven to **350°F**.
- Stir together **2 cups almond flour**, **1/4 cup cocoa**, **1/4 cup melted coconut oil**, **3 tbsp maple syrup**, and **1/2 tsp salt** in a bowl until evenly combined.
- Press mixture firmly into the bottom and up the sides of a **9-inch tart pan**.

**2. Bake the Crust**
- Bake for **12 minutes** until dry and firm to the touch.
- Transfer to wire rack and **cool completely**.

**3. Prepare Chocolate Filling**
- Roughly chop **1 lb dark chocolate** and place in a heat-safe bowl.
- Heat **1.5 cups coconut milk** in a saucepan until simmering (do not boil).
- Pour hot coconut milk over the chocolate. Let sit for **30 seconds**.
- Slowly stir until chocolate is completely melted and glossy.
- Stir in **1 tsp vanilla** and **1/4 tsp salt**.

### Finish: 2 hours (Chilling)

**4. Final Assembly**
- Pour filling into the cooled crust.
- Smooth top with a spoon.
- **Refrigerate for at least 2 hours** (or overnight) until firm.

**5. Serving**
- Sprinkle with **flaky sea salt** just before slicing.
- **Portion size:** Cut into 12 wedges.

---

## Verification Checks

- Total tart weight should be approx 2 lbs.
- Filling should be firm and not "runny" after 2 hours.
- If too soft, refrigerate for an additional hour.

---

## Storage & Reheating

**Storage:**
- Keep refrigerated.
- **Max 5 days** in an airtight container.
- **Freezable:** Can be frozen for up to 1 month. Thaw in fridge before serving.

**Reheating:**
- Serve chilled. Do not reheat (the filling will melt).
