Honey Garlic Salmon & Roasted Asparagus

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-Free
Week 3 • Monday Fresh Cook (Fish Priority)
Cuisine: Healthy/Seafood Style: Fast Fresh Cook Prep: 10 min Cook: 15 min Total: 25 min
Protocol Note: Fish is always cooked on Monday to ensure maximum freshness after Sunday shopping.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs Salmon Fillet (Wild-caught preferred)
  • 1 bunch Asparagus, trimmed
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • Honey Garlic Glaze:
  • 3 tbsp Honey
  • 2 tbsp Tamari (GF)
  • 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp Ginger, minced

Instructions

Step 1: Roast Asparagus (12 min)

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Toss 1 bunch asparagus with 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper on a sheet pan.
  3. Roast for 10-12 minutes until tender-crisp.

Step 2: Pan-Sear Salmon (10 min)

  1. Whisk together 3 tbsp honey, 2 tbsp tamari, 1 tbsp lemon juice, 3 cloves garlic, and 1/2 tsp ginger.
  2. Cut 1.5 lbs salmon into portions. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-high. Add a splash of oil.
  4. Sear salmon skin-side down for 4-5 minutes. Flip and sear for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Pour glaze into the pan and let it bubble for 1 minute until thickened and coating the fish.

Step 3: Serve

  1. Serve salmon drizzled with extra glaze alongside roasted asparagus. Optional: Add a squeeze of fresh lemon.
