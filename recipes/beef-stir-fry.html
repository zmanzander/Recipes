🔥 Tuesday: Beef & Yellow Pepper Stir-fry

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-Free
Time: 20 mins
Storage: 3 days in fridge
Hanna Solo Dinner

Ingredients (Hanna Solo)

  • 0.5 lb (8 oz) Lean Ground Beef
  • 1 medium Yellow Bell Pepper (thinly sliced)
  • 1/2 Zucchini (peeled, half-moons)
  • 2 tbsp Coconut Aminos (soy-free)
  • 1 tsp Fresh Ginger (grated)
  • 1 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

Heat 1 tbsp garlic oil in a skillet over Medium-High Heat.
Add the 8 oz ground beef. Brown until cooked through.
Instruction Update: Once beef is browned, add 1 tsp ginger and a pinch of salt. Stir for 1 min.
Add the sliced yellow pepper and zucchini half-moons.
Stir-fry over High Heat for 4-5 mins until vegetables are tender-crisp.
Pour in 2 tbsp coconut aminos. Toss everything together and cook for 1 more min.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)

Hanna: Serve the entire portion immediately.
