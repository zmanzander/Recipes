Herb-Roasted Chicken & Vegetables

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
⏱️ Prep: 20 mins | 🔥 Cook: 75 mins | 📦 Makes: 4 Portions

Ingredients

  • Whole Chicken: 1 Whole Chicken (approx. 4.5 - 5 lbs)
  • Carrots: 4 large Carrots, cut into 1/2-inch thick coins
  • Zucchini: 3 medium Zucchini, peeled and sliced into 1/2-inch half-moons
  • Yellow Bell Peppers: 2 Yellow Bell Peppers, cut into 1/2-inch strips
  • Oil: 4 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil (divided)
  • Herbs: 2 tbsp Fresh Rosemary and 2 tbsp Fresh Thyme, finely chopped
  • Lemon: 1 Lemon, halved
  • Seasoning: Sea Salt and Black Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prep Oven: Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Season Chicken: Pat the 1 Whole Chicken (approx. 4.5 - 5 lbs) dry with paper towels. Rub the skin with 2 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil, then coat evenly with the 2 tbsp chopped rosemary, 2 tbsp chopped thyme, sea salt, and black pepper. Place 2 lemon halves inside the chicken cavity.
  3. Prep Vegetables: In a large bowl, toss the 4 large sliced carrots, 3 peeled and sliced zucchini, and 2 sliced yellow bell peppers with the remaining 2 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Arrange & Roast: Place the chicken in a large roasting pan. Arrange the vegetables in a single layer around the chicken.
  5. Roast: Place the pan in the oven and roast for 1 hour 15 minutes, or until the thickest part of the thigh reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
  6. Rest & Carve: Remove from the oven and let the chicken rest for 10 minutes before carving. This ensures the meat stays juicy for your lunches.

Portioning (Cooked Weight)

  • Zander Sunday Dinner & Monday Lunch: Approx. 9 oz cooked chicken (white or dark meat) + 1.5 cups roasted vegetables.
  • Hanna Sunday Dinner & Monday Lunch: Approx. 5.5 oz cooked chicken + 1 cup roasted vegetables.

Medical Compliance

✅ Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Egg-free, Soy-free.
🚫 No garlic/onion pieces (infused oil only).
🟨 Yellow bell peppers only.
