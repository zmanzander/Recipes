Turkey & Spaghetti Squash Boats

⏱️ Prep: 15 mins | 🔥 Cook: 45 mins | 📦 Makes: 4 Portions

Ingredients

  • Ground Turkey: 38.6 oz raw total (Zander 2x 12oz, Hanna 2x 7.3oz)
  • Spaghetti Squash: 2 medium Spaghetti Squash
  • Yellow Bell Peppers: 2 Yellow Bell Peppers, cut into 1/4-inch dice
  • Spinach: 2 cups Fresh Spinach
  • Green Onions: 1/2 cup Green Onion tops (green part only), finely chopped
  • Oil: 2 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil (divided)
  • Spices: 1 tbsp Cumin, 1 tbsp Paprika, and Sea Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Prep Squash: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Cut the 2 medium spaghetti squash in half lengthwise and scrape out the seeds.
  2. Roast Squash: Lightly brush the inside of each squash half with a small amount of garlic-infused olive oil and a pinch of salt. Place them cut-side down on a baking sheet and roast for 40 minutes, or until the skin is easily pierced with a fork.
  3. Cook Turkey: While the squash is roasting, heat 1 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the 38.6 oz ground turkey and season with 1 tbsp cumin, 1 tbsp paprika, and sea salt. Cook for 8-10 minutes, breaking the meat into small crumbles until fully browned.
  4. Sauté Vegetables: Add the 2 diced yellow bell peppers and 1/2 cup chopped green onion tops to the turkey. Continue cooking over medium heat for 5 minutes until the peppers are just tender.
  5. Add Spinach: Stir in the 2 cups fresh spinach and cook for 1-2 minutes until wilted. Remove from heat.
  6. Assemble Boats: Once the squash is roasted, use a fork to scrape the internal flesh into "noodles" (keeping them inside the shell). Divide the turkey mixture evenly among the 4 squash halves.

Portioning (Cooked Volume)

  • Zander Tuesday Dinner & Wednesday Lunch: One full squash boat (approx. 9 oz cooked turkey + half of one medium squash).
  • Hanna Tuesday Dinner & Wednesday Lunch: One full squash boat (approx. 5.5 oz cooked turkey + half of one medium squash).

Medical Compliance

✅ Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Egg-free, Soy-free.
🚫 No garlic/onion pieces (infused oil and tops only).
🟨 Yellow bell peppers only.
