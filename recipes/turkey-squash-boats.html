Monday: Turkey Spaghetti Squash Boats

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Time: 50 mins
Storage: 3 days in fridge
Hanna Solo Dinner

Ingredients (Hanna Solo)

  • 1 small Spaghetti Squash
  • 0.5 lb (8 oz) Ground Turkey (93% lean)
  • 1/2 bunch Green Onion Tops (sliced)
  • 1 cup Spinach (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • 2 tbsp Low-FODMAP Salsa (Fody brand)
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

Preheat oven to 400°F. Cut the spaghetti squash in half lengthwise, remove seeds, and brush with 1 tbsp garlic oil and salt. Roast face-down for 40 mins.
While squash roasts, heat remaining 1 tbsp oil in a small skillet over Medium-High Heat.
Add the 8 oz ground turkey. Brown until cooked through.
Instruction Update: Once turkey is browned, add the chopped spinach and green onion tops. Stir until wilted. Season with salt and pepper.
Once squash is done, scrape the insides with a fork to create "spaghetti." Mix the turkey/spinach into the squash halves.
Top with the 2 tbsp salsa for moisture.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)

Hanna: Serve both halves as a single solo dinner.
