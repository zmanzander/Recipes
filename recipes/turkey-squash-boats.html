Turkey & Spaghetti Squash Boats

Prep time: 15 mins | Cook time: 45 mins

Ingredients (4 Portions)

  • Ground Turkey: 38.6oz raw total (Zander 2x 12oz, Hanna 2x 7.3oz)
  • 2 medium Spaghetti Squash
  • 2 Yellow Bell Peppers (diced)
  • 2 cups Spinach (fresh)
  • 1/2 cup Green Onion tops (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Cumin
  • 1 tbsp Paprika
  • Sea salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Cut spaghetti squash in half, remove seeds, brush with oil/salt, and roast face down for 40 mins.
  2. In a large skillet, brown ground turkey with cumin, paprika, and salt.
  3. Add diced yellow bell peppers and green onion tops. Cook for 5 mins.
  4. Stir in spinach until wilted.
  5. Scrape squash flesh with a fork to create "noodles" but keep in the shell. Stuff with turkey mixture.

Portioning

  • Tuesday Dinner: Zander (12oz raw), Hanna (7.3oz raw)
  • Wednesday Lunch: Zander (12oz raw), Hanna (7.3oz raw)

Medical Compliance

Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Egg-free, Soy-free. No garlic/onion pieces. Green onion tops used.
