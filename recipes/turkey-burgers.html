Turkey Burgers with Yukon Gold Fries

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Week 3 • Thursday Fresh Cook
Style: Fast Fresh Cook Prep: 15 min Cook: 25 min Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs Ground Turkey (93/7)
  • 4 large Yukon Gold Potatoes (cut into fries)
  • 1 tsp Smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp Smoked paprika
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Lettuce/Greens (for serving)
  • Avocado (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Roast Yukon Gold Potato Fries (25 min)

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Cut 4 Yukon Gold potatoes into thin fries. Toss with olive oil, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roast for 20-25 minutes until crisp.

Step 2: Cook Turkey Burgers (15 min)

  1. Form 1.5 lbs ground turkey into patties (4 for Zander, 4 smaller for Hanna). Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a large skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Cook for 5-6 minutes per side until 165°F internal.

Step 3: Serve

  1. Serve burgers over salad greens with Yukon Gold potato fries on the side. Top with avocado if available.

Portion Summary (Cooked)

Alexander: 10 oz turkey (2 patties) + 1.5 cups Yukon Gold potato fries

Hanna: 6 oz turkey (1 large patty) + 1 cup Yukon Gold potato fries
