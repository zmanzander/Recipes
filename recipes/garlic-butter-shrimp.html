Garlic Butter Shrimp (New & Improved)

Gluten-FreeEgg-Free
Week 3 • Wednesday Fresh Cook
Cuisine: Seafood Style: Fast Fresh Cook Prep: 10 min Cook: 10 min Total: 20 min
Recipe Update: Increased garlic, added lemon zest and red pepper flakes for a major flavor boost over the original version.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs Large Shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tbsp Butter (Kerrygold)
  • 6 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 Lemon (zested and juiced)
  • 1 tsp Red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup Chicken broth (or dry white wine)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Jasmine Rice (for serving)
  • 1 bunch Asparagus or Broccoli

Instructions

Step 1: Prep & Sear (5 min)

  1. Pat 1.5 lbs shrimp dry. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add 1 tbsp oil.
  3. Cook shrimp for 1-2 minutes per side until pink and opaque. Remove and set aside.

Step 2: Make the Sauce (5 min)

  1. In the same skillet, reduce heat to medium and add 4 tbsp butter.
  2. Once melted, add 6 minced garlic cloves and 1 tsp red pepper flakes. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Whisk in 1/2 cup chicken broth, lemon zest, and 1 tbsp lemon juice. Simmer for 2 minutes to reduce slightly.

Step 3: Finish & Serve

  1. Return shrimp to the pan. Toss to coat in the sauce.
  2. Stir in fresh parsley.
  3. Serve over jasmine rice with a side of steamed asparagus.
