Oven Roasted Chicken with Peaches

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Protein: ChickenStyle: Oven RoastedTime: Prep: 20 min | Cook: 60 min

🛡️ Medical Protocol Adaptations (Low-FODMAP)

  • Shallots: Replace with the green tops of chives or green onions to maintain flavor while staying Low-FODMAP.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (3-4 pounds, spatchcocked)
  • 1/4 cup garlic-infused olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Celtic sea salt
  • 4 peaches, sliced (use firm-ripe)
  • 1/2 cup green onion tops (sliced)
  • 6 sprigs fresh thyme
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prep: Position chicken breast side down and cut out backbone. Flip over and press on chest bone to flatten (spatchcock).
  2. Season: Rub with infused oil, season with salt, then scatter peaches, green onion tops, and thyme around the chicken in a heavy roasting pan or cast iron skillet.
  3. Roast: Preheat oven to 400°F. Roast for 55-60 minutes until the internal temperature reaches 165°F.
  4. Serve: Serve with roasted peaches and pan sauce. Garnish with fresh parsley.

Portioning Guide

Alexander: 1/2 chicken + generous serving of peaches.

Hanna: 1/4 chicken + serving of peaches.

Notes

Spatchcocking allows for even cooking and extra crispy skin. If peaches aren't in season, frozen (thawed) peaches work well too.
