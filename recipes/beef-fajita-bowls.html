Low-FODMAP Beef Fajita Bowls

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Time: 45 mins
Style: One Pan Stir-fry (Sat + Sun Lunch)

Ingredients (Shared Batch: 4 portions)

  • 3 lbs Flank Steak or Sirloin (thinly sliced across grain)
  • 4 total Yellow Bell Peppers (sliced into strips)
  • 1/2 cup Green Onion tops (sliced)
  • 4 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil (divided)
  • 1 tbsp Chili Powder (ensure Low-FODMAP)
  • 1 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp Sea Salt
  • 3 cups Cooked Jasmine Rice (for serving)

Step-by-Step Instructions

In a large bowl, toss the 3 lbs sliced beef with 2 tbsp garlic-infused oil, chili powder, cumin, paprika, and salt.
Heat 1 tbsp garlic oil in a large skillet or wok over High Heat. Sear beef in batches until browned and just cooked through (2-3 mins). Set aside.
Add the final 1 tbsp garlic oil to the pan. Sauté 4 sliced yellow peppers for 5-7 mins until tender-crisp.
Return beef to the pan, add 1/2 cup green onion tops, and toss together for 1-2 mins.
Serve over cooked jasmine rice. Save half for Sunday lunch.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)

Alexander (2 meals): 10 oz cooked beef/pepper mix + 1.5 cups rice per meal.

Hanna (2 meals): 6 oz cooked beef/pepper mix + 1 cup rice per meal.
