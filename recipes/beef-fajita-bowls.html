Low-FODMAP Beef Fajita Bowls

Week 3 • Mon-Wed Lunch Prep + Thu Fresh Cook
Style: 3-Day Meal Prep + Fresh Cook (Low-FODMAP) Prep: 15 min Cook: 25 min Strategy: Sunday prep (Mon-Wed), Wednesday night fresh cook (Thu)

🥘 PART 1: Sunday Prep (Mon-Wed Lunches)

Makes: 6 portions (3 days x 2 people)

Ingredients (Sunday Prep)

  • 4 lbs flank or sirloin steak, sliced thin against the grain
  • 3 bell peppers (mix of red, yellow, orange), sliced into strips
  • 1.5 bunches Spring Onions (GREEN PARTS ONLY - save 0.5 bunch for Thursday)
  • 2.5 cups dry jasmine rice
  • Garlic-infused olive oil
  • Seasoning: 1.5 tbsp Cumin, 1 tbsp Chili powder (check for garlic/onion), 1 tsp Smoked paprika, 1 tsp salt, 3/4 tsp black pepper.

Instructions (Sunday Prep)

  1. Cook rice: Rinse 2.5 cups dry jasmine rice 2-3 times. Add to a pot with 3.75 cups water. Bring to a boil, reduce to simmer, cover for 18-20 minutes. Fluff and let cool.
  2. Prep vegetables: Slice 3 bell peppers into strips. Slice the green tops only of 1.5 bunches of spring onions.
  3. Season steak: Mix 1.5 tbsp cumin, 1 tbsp chili powder, 1 tsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp salt, and 3/4 tsp black pepper. Toss 4 lbs thinly sliced steak with seasoning and 1 tbsp garlic-infused oil.
  4. Sear steak: Heat 1 tbsp garlic-infused oil in a large skillet over high heat. Cook steak in batches for 2-3 minutes per side until charred. Remove and set aside.
  5. Sauté vegetables: Add another splash of oil to the pan. Cook peppers and spring onion greens for 5-8 minutes until softened and charred.
  6. Portion & Store (6 containers):
    • Alexander (3 containers): 8-10 oz cooked steak + 1.5 cups rice + 1.5 cups vegetables per container.
    • Hanna (3 containers): 5-6 oz cooked steak + 1 cup rice + 1 cup vegetables per container.

🔥 PART 2: Wednesday Night Fresh Cook (Thursday Lunch)

Timing: Cook Wednesday evening after dinner. Store in fridge overnight for Thursday lunch.

Makes: 2 portions (1 for Alexander, 1 for Hanna)

Ingredients (Wednesday Fresh Cook)

  • 0.75 lb steak, sliced thin against the grain
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced into strips
  • 0.5 bunch Spring Onions (GREEN PARTS ONLY)
  • 0.75 cup dry jasmine rice
  • Garlic-infused olive oil
  • Seasoning: 1/2 tbsp cumin, 1/2 tbsp chili powder, 1/4 tsp smoked paprika, 1/4 tsp salt, pinch black pepper.

Instructions (Wednesday Evening)

  1. Cook rice: Rinse 0.75 cup dry rice. Cook with 1.125 cups water for 18-20 minutes.
  2. Prep & season: Slice 1 bell pepper and spring onion greens. Toss 0.75 lb sliced steak with seasoning blend and garlic-infused oil.
  3. Sear steak: High heat, 2-3 minutes per side until charred.
  4. Sauté vegetables: Cook peppers and spring onion greens for 5 minutes until tender.
  5. Portion into 2 containers:
    • Alexander: 8-10 oz steak + 1.5 cups rice + heavy vegetables.
    • Hanna: 5-6 oz steak + 1 cup rice + moderate vegetables.
  6. Store in fridge overnight. Reheat Thursday morning or at lunch.

Target Weights (Cooked)

Alexander: 8-10 oz steak + 1.5 cups rice + heavy vegetables per meal.

Hanna: 5-6 oz steak + 1 cup rice + moderate vegetables per meal.

Why This Strategy?

Food Safety: 3-day max storage keeps everything fresh and safe. Thursday's meal is only 1 night old when eaten.

Minimal Extra Work: Wednesday's fresh cook takes only 20-25 minutes—much faster than a full Sunday prep.

Better Quality: Thursday's lunch tastes fresher than 4-day-old meal prep.

Reheating

Oven: 350°F for 10 minutes. Top with fresh cilantro and lime after reheating.

Important: Verify your chili powder is pure (no added onion/garlic powder) to stay Low-FODMAP.
