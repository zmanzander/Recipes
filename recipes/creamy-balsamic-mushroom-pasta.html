Creamy Balsamic Mushroom Pasta with Ground Beef

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Protein: Ground BeefStyle: One PotTime: Prep: 10 min | Cook: 25 min

🛡️ Medical Protocol Adaptations (Low-FODMAP)

  • Aromatics: Replaced onion and garlic with 3 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil and 1/4 cup green onion tops.
  • Mushrooms: Swapped portobello/button mushrooms for Oyster Mushrooms (Low-FODMAP safe).
  • Pasta: Use 100% Cassava or other grain-free/GF pasta per protocol.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef (Grass-fed preferred)
  • 8 oz Oyster Mushrooms (sliced)
  • 3 cups Fresh Spinach
  • 1/4 cup Green Onion tops (sliced)
  • 3 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • 1/4 cup Balsamic Vinegar
  • 1/4 cup Full-fat Coconut Milk (additive-free)
  • 1/4 cup Hot Water
  • 1 tsp Dried Thyme
  • 1 tsp Sea Salt
  • 2 tbsp Tapioca Starch (mixed with 3 tbsp water for slurry)
  • 6 oz Gluten-Free/Cassava Pasta

Instructions

  1. Pasta: Boil pasta to al dente, strain, and set aside.
  2. Brown Beef: Heat a large pot over medium-high heat with the infused oil. Add the ground beef, salt, and thyme. Cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon.
  3. Mushrooms: Add the oyster mushrooms and green onion tops. Sauté for 5-7 minutes until mushrooms are softened and slightly golden.
  4. Sauce: Stir in the balsamic vinegar, hot water, and coconut milk. Bring to a gentle simmer.
  5. Thicken: Fold in the spinach until wilted. Stir in the tapioca slurry quickly until the sauce thickens.
  6. Combine: Remove from heat and fold in the cooked pasta. Taste and adjust salt.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)

Alexander: 10 oz beef/sauce mixture + 1.5 cups pasta.

Hanna: 6 oz beef/sauce mixture + 1 cup pasta.
