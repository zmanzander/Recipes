Creamy Balsamic Mushroom Pasta with Ground Beef

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Time: 35 mins
Style: One Pot

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 8 oz Oyster Mushrooms (sliced)
  • 3 cups Fresh Spinach
  • 6 oz Gluten-Free/Cassava Pasta
  • 1/4 cup Balsamic Vinegar
  • 1/4 cup Full-fat Coconut Milk
  • Garlic-infused Olive Oil, Thyme, Sea Salt
  • Tapioca starch (for slurry)

Step-by-Step Instructions

Boil pasta to al dente, strain, and set aside.
Brown the ground beef in a large pot with infused oil, salt, and thyme.
Add oyster mushrooms and green onion tops; sauté 5-7 mins.
Stir in balsamic vinegar, water, and coconut milk. Simmer.
Fold in spinach to wilt, then add tapioca slurry to thicken.
Combine with cooked pasta and serve.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)

Alexander: 10 oz beef/sauce mix + 1.5 cups pasta. Hanna: 6 oz mix + 1 cup pasta.
