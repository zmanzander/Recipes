Crispy Chicken Thighs with Lemon-Caper Sauce

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Time: 45 mins
Storage: Best fresh
Portions: 2 (Zander & Hanna)

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs Chicken Thighs (Bone-in, skin-on)
  • 1 lb Green Beans (trimmed)
  • 1 tbsp Capers (drained)
  • 2 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup Chicken Stock (Low-FODMAP/GF)
  • 2 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • Fresh Parsley (chopped)
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

Preheat oven to 400°F. Pat 1.5 lbs chicken thighs dry and season with salt and pepper.
Heat 1 tbsp garlic oil in an oven-safe skillet over Medium Heat.
Place thighs skin-side down. Cook for 10-12 mins without moving them, until skin is golden brown and very crispy.
Flip the thighs and transfer the skillet to the oven. Roast at 400°F for 10-15 mins until internal temp reaches 165°F. Remove chicken and let rest.
Carefully drain most of the fat from the pan. Add 1/2 cup chicken stock, 2 tbsp lemon juice, and 1 tbsp capers.
Scrape the bottom of the pan and simmer over Medium-High Heat for 3-5 mins until the sauce reduces by half. Stir in fresh parsley.
While sauce reduces, sauté the 1 lb green beans in a separate pan with 1 tbsp garlic oil for 5-7 mins.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)

Alexander: 9-10 oz meat weight (usually 3 thighs) + half the green beans.

Hanna: 5.5-6 oz meat weight (usually 2 thighs) + half the green beans.
