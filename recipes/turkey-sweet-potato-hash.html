Turkey & Yukon Gold Potato Hash

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Cuisine: American Style: One Pot Prep: 20 min Cook: 25 min Stores: 3-4 days

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs ground turkey (93/7)
  • 4 large Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and diced (1/2 inch cubes)
  • 1/2 cup green onion tops (green parts only), sliced
  • 2 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Peel and dice Yukon Gold potatoes into 1/2-inch cubes. Microwave for 5 minutes to par-cook.
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, add 1 tbsp oil. Cook ground turkey, breaking it up, until no longer pink (8-10 min). Remove and set aside.
  3. Add remaining oil to the same pan. Add green onion tops and cook 5 minutes until softened. Add garlic and cook 1 minute.
  4. Add par-cooked Yukon Gold potatoes to the pan. Cook 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until browned and softened.
  5. Return turkey to pan. Add all spices. Stir well and cook 3-4 minutes.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.

Portion Sizes

Alexander: 1.5 cups + side salad

Wife: 1 cup + side salad

Notes

Stores 3-4 days. Freezes well for up to 2 months. Reheats in microwave 3 min or skillet 5 min. Can substitute ground beef (90/10) or ground chicken.
