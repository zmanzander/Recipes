Pan-Seared Salmon with Bok Choy

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
⏱️ Prep: 10 mins | 🔥 Cook: 15 mins | 📦 Makes: 1 Portion (Zander)

Ingredients

  • Salmon Fillet: 12 oz raw total
  • Bok Choy: 1 head Bok Choy (use green leafy parts only)
  • Rice: 0.75 cups White Rice (dry)
  • Oil: 1 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil (divided)
  • Ginger: 1 tsp Fresh Ginger, finely grated
  • Lemon: 1/2 Lemon, juiced
  • Herbs: 1 tbsp Fresh Dill, finely chopped
  • Seasoning: Sea Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook Rice: Start by cooking the 0.75 cups dry white rice according to package instructions. Set aside once finished.
  2. Season Salmon: Pat the 12 oz salmon fillet dry. Season both sides with sea salt, 1 tbsp chopped fresh dill, and the juice of 1/2 a lemon.
  3. Sear Salmon: Heat 1/2 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil in a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Place the salmon in the pan (skin-side down if applicable) and sear for 4-5 minutes per side, until the flesh is opaque and flakes easily with a fork. Remove from pan and let rest.
  4. Sauté Bok Choy: In a separate small skillet, heat the remaining 1/2 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil over medium heat. Add the 1 tsp grated fresh ginger and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant. Add the 1 head of green bok choy leaves and sauté for 3-4 minutes until wilted and tender.
  5. Plate: Serve the approx. 9 oz cooked salmon over the approx. 1.5 cups cooked rice with the ginger bok choy on the side.

Portioning (Cooked Weight)

  • Zander Monday Dinner: Approx. 9 oz cooked salmon + 1.5 cups cooked rice + all prepped bok choy.

Medical Compliance

✅ Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Egg-free, Soy-free.
🚫 No garlic/onion pieces (infused oil only).
✅ Low-FODMAP (Bok choy greens and ginger are safe).
