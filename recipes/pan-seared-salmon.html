Pan-Seared Salmon with Bok Choy

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Time: 25 mins
Style: Pan Seared (Saturday Dinner)

Ingredients (Shared Batch: 2 portions)

  • 1.5 lbs Salmon Fillets (skin-on preferred)
  • 2 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • 1 large bunch Bok Choy (chopped, whites and greens separated)
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tsp Sea Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper

Step-by-Step Instructions

Pat 1.5 lbs salmon fillets dry with paper towels. Season with salt and pepper.
Heat 1 tbsp garlic-infused oil in a large skillet over Medium-High Heat. Place salmon skin-side down. Cook for 5-6 mins until skin is crispy.
Flip the salmon and cook for another 3-4 mins until just cooked through. Remove and set aside.
Add the remaining 1 tbsp garlic oil to the skillet. Sauté the chopped bok choy whites for 3-4 mins.
Add the bok choy greens and sauté for 2-3 mins until wilted. Finish with lemon juice and a pinch of salt.
Serve salmon immediately with the bok choy.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)

Alexander: 10 oz cooked salmon + half the bok choy.

Hanna: 6 oz cooked salmon + half the bok choy.
