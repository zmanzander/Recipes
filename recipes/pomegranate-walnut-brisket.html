Pomegranate-Walnut Brisket

A stunning, impressive main course perfect for entertaining and large gatherings.

Protein: Beef (Brisket)
Servings: 8-10
Prep Time: 30 min (plus 24h marinating)
Cook Time: 5-6 hours

🛡️ Medical Protocol Adaptations (Low-FODMAP)

  • Garlic: Replace the whole head of garlic in the marinade with 1/4 cup garlic-infused extra virgin olive oil.
  • Honey: Replace honey in the pomegranate sauce with maple syrup.
  • Gremolata: Omit raw garlic; double the lemon zest and add fresh chives for flavor depth.

Ingredients

The Brisket & Marinade

  • 7 lb Beef Brisket (trimmed)
  • 1/4 cup Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Rosemary (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Thyme (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Kosher Salt
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper

    • Instructions

    1. Marinate: Rub brisket with oil, herbs, salt, and pepper. Wrap tightly and refrigerate for 24-48 hours.
    2. Roast: Preheat oven to 300°F. Place brisket in a heavy roasting pan, cover tightly with foil. Roast for 5-6 hours until fork-tender.
    3. Sauce: Reduce 2 cups pomegranate juice with 1/2 cup toasted crushed walnuts and 2 tbsp maple syrup until syrupy.
    4. Gremolata: Mix 1/2 cup chopped parsley, 1 tbsp lemon zest, and 2 tbsp chopped chives.
    5. Serve: Slice brisket against the grain, top with sauce and fresh gremolata.
