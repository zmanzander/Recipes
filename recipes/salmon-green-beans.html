🐟 Sunday: Pan-Seared Salmon & Green Beans

Time: 25 mins
Storage: Eat immediately
Hanna Solo Dinner

Ingredients (Hanna Solo)

  • 0.5 lb (8 oz) Salmon Fillet
  • 1/2 lb Green Beans (trimmed)
  • 2 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • 1/2 Lemon (sliced)
  • 1 tsp Fresh Dill (chopped)
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

Season the 8 oz salmon with salt and pepper.
Heat 1 tbsp garlic oil in a small skillet over Medium-High Heat.
Place salmon skin-side down. Cook for 4-5 mins until skin is crispy. Flip and cook for another 3-4 mins until desired doneness. Finish with lemon juice and 1 tsp dill. Remove salmon.
Add remaining 1 tbsp oil and the 1/2 lb green beans to the same skillet.
Sauté over Medium Heat for 5-7 mins until tender-crisp. Season with salt.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)

Hanna: Serve the entire fillet with all green beans.
