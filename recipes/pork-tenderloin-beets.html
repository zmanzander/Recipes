Pork Tenderloin with Pickled Beets & Goat Cheese

Gluten-FreeEgg-Free
Week 2 • Monday Dinner
Cuisine: Mediterranean/American Style: Sheet Pan + Assembly Prep: 10 min Cook: 25 min Total: 35 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs pork tenderloin
  • Hanna's pickled beets — 1 cup, sliced
  • 4 oz goat cheese, crumbled
  • 4 cups mixed greens (arugula or spring mix)
  • 1/2 cup walnuts, toasted
  • 1 small shallot, minced (light onion)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil (for pork)
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • Balsamic dressing: 3 tbsp balsamic vinegar, 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp Dijon mustard

Instructions (Quantities Inline)

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Season pork: Mix 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp dried thyme, 1/2 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp pepper. Rub all over 1.5 lbs pork tenderloin.
  3. Sear pork: Heat an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat. Sear tenderloin for 3 minutes per side until browned.
  4. Roast: Transfer skillet to the oven. Roast for 15 minutes until internal temp reaches 145°F.
  5. Rest: Remove from oven, tent with foil, and rest for 5 minutes.
  6. Make dressing: Whisk 3 tbsp balsamic vinegar, 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp Dijon mustard, and 1 minced shallot together.
  7. Toast walnuts: In a dry pan over medium heat, toast 1/2 cup walnuts for 3-4 minutes until fragrant.
  8. Slice pork: Cut the rested tenderloin into medallions against the grain.
  9. Assemble plates: Create a bed of 4 cups mixed greens. Top with the sliced pork, 1 cup sliced pickled beets, 4 oz crumbled goat cheese, and the toasted walnuts. Drizzle with the balsamic dressing.

Portion Sizes

Alexander: 6 oz pork + 2 cups greens + 1/2 cup beets + 2 oz goat cheese

Hanna: 5 oz pork + 1.5 cups greens + 1/2 cup beets + 1.5 oz goat cheese

Notes

⭐ Special: Features Hanna's homemade pickled beets!

The sweet-tangy beets pair beautifully with the savory pork and creamy goat cheese.

Keep shallot minimal — just enough for flavor without overwhelming.
