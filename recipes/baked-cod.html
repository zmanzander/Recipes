Baked Salmon with Lemon Caper Sauce & Roasted Broccoli

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Protein: Salmon Fillets Style: Sheet Pan + Pan (Wed Dinner) Prep: 10 min | Cook: 25 min

Ingredients (Shared Batch: 2 portions)

  • 1.5 lbs Salmon Fillets (fresh!)
  • 2 large heads Broccoli (cut into florets)
  • 4 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil (divided)
  • 3 tbsp Capers (drained)
  • 2 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • Sea Salt and Black Pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

Preheat oven to 425°F. Toss 2 large heads broccoli florets with 2 tbsp garlic-infused oil and salt. Roast for 20 mins until charred.
Pat 1.5 lbs salmon fillets dry. Season with 1 tsp paprika and salt.
Heat 1 tbsp garlic-infused oil in a large skillet over Medium-High Heat. Cook salmon for 4 mins per side until opaque.
Remove salmon. Add the final 1 tbsp garlic oil, 3 tbsp capers, and 2 tbsp lemon juice to the pan. Swirl for 1 min.
Spoon the lemon-caper sauce over the salmon and serve with roasted broccoli.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)

Alexander: 10 oz salmon + half the broccoli. Hanna: 6 oz salmon + half the broccoli.
