Baked Cod with Lemon Caper Sauce & Roasted Broccoli

Gluten-FreeEgg-Free
Week 2 • Sunday Dinner
Cuisine: Mediterranean Style: Sheet Pan + Pan Prep: 10 min Cook: 25 min Total: 35 min

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs cod fillets (buy fresh Sunday!)
  • 2 large heads broccoli, cut into florets
  • 3 tbsp olive oil (divided)
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp capers, drained
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions (Quantities Inline)

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Prep broccoli: Toss 2 large heads of broccoli florets with 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread on a baking sheet.
  3. Roast broccoli: Roast for 20 minutes until tender and slightly charred.
  4. Season cod: Pat 1.5 lbs cod fillets dry. Sprinkle both sides with 1 tsp paprika, salt, and pepper.
  5. Cook cod: Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Add cod and cook 4 minutes per side until flaky and opaque.
  6. Make sauce: Remove cod from pan. Add 3 tbsp butter, 2 minced garlic cloves, 3 tbsp drained capers, and 2 tbsp lemon juice to the hot pan. Swirl 1 minute until garlic is fragrant and sauce is combined.
  7. Plate: Spoon the sauce over the cod. Serve with the roasted broccoli.

Portion Sizes

Alexander: 8 oz cod + sauce + 2 cups broccoli

Hanna: 6 oz cod + sauce + 1.5 cups broccoli

Notes

⚠️ Buy cod fresh on Sunday — cook same night for best quality.

Best fresh, not ideal for meal prep. This is your Sunday night treat before meal prepping chicken bowls.

If cod is unavailable, substitute halibut or sea bass.
