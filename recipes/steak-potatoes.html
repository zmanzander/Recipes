Steak & Potatoes

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Week 6 • Sunday Shared Dinner
Cuisine: Classic American Style: Simple Fresh Cook Prep: 15 min Cook: 30 min Total: 45 min

Ingredients

  • Steak: 1.5 lbs Sirloin or Ribeye (boneless)
  • Potatoes: 1.5 lbs Yukon Gold Potatoes (cut into 1-inch cubes)
  • Side: 1 lb Carrots (sliced into coins)
  • Garlic Butter (Mock): 4 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil, 1 tbsp fresh rosemary (chopped), 1 tbsp fresh thyme (chopped), sea salt, and black pepper.

Instructions

  1. Roast Potatoes: Preheat oven to 425°F. Toss 1.5 lbs diced Yukon Gold potatoes with 2 tbsp garlic oil, salt, and pepper. Roast for 25-30 mins until crispy.
  2. Prep Carrots: In a separate pan (or same sheet pan if room), toss 1 lb carrot coins with 1 tbsp garlic oil and sea salt. Roast for 20-25 mins until tender.
  3. Sear Steak: Pat 1.5 lbs steak dry. Season generously with salt and pepper. Heat 1 tbsp garlic oil in a heavy skillet over medium-high heat. Sear steak for 4-5 mins per side (medium-rare).
  4. Herb Finish: During the last 2 mins of cooking, add 1 tbsp rosemary and 1 tbsp thyme to the pan. Spoon the infused oil over the steak continuously.
  5. Rest: Remove steak and let rest for 5-10 mins before slicing. Serve with roasted potatoes and carrots.

Portion Guide (Cooked)

Alexander: 10 oz steak + 1.5 cups potatoes + 1 cup carrots.

Hanna: 6 oz steak + 1 cup potatoes + 1 cup carrots.

Medical Compliance

✅ Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Soy-free, Egg-free.
🚫 No onion/garlic pieces (infused oil only).
🟨 Clean whole foods focused.
