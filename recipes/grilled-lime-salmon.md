# Grilled Lime Salmon with Avocado-Mango Salsa

**Medical Status:** Validated Low-FODMAP (Mar 2026)
  - 🚫 **NO Garlic:** Replace 3 cloves crushed garlic with **2 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil** in marinade.
  - 🚫 **NO Red Onion:** Omit red onion from salsa. Replace with **1/4 cup Green Onion tops** (green parts only).
  - 🚫 **NO Red Bell Pepper:** Replace 3/4 cup red bell pepper with **3/4 cup Yellow Bell Pepper**.
  - 🚫 **Mango Note:** Mango is high-FODMAP in large amounts. Limit serving to **1/4 cup salsa per person**.
  - 🚫 **Avocado Note:** Avocado is high-FODMAP in large amounts. Serving size (1/4 avocado per person) is at the threshold; monitor for sensitivity.
  - ✅ Safe substitutes: Coconut milk, coconut water, lime juice, cilantro are all safe.

## Step-by-Step Instructions (For 4 Portions)

### Prep & Marinate: 45 minutes

**1. Marinate the Salmon (2.2 lbs)**
- In a large glass baking dish, whisk together:
  - **3 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil** (Replaces garlic cloves + base oil)
  - **2 tsp Lime Zest**
  - **3 tbsp Fresh Lime Juice**
  - **1 tsp Salt**
  - **1/2 tsp Black Pepper**
- Place **2.2 lbs salmon fillets** in the dish. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes. Flip once halfway through.

**2. Cook Coconut Rice (1.5 cups dry)**
- In a medium saucepan, combine:
  - **1.5 cups dry Jasmine Rice** (rinsed)
  - **1.5 cups Coconut Water**
  - **1.25 cups Full-fat Coconut Milk**
  - **1/2 tsp Salt**
- Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and cover.
- Simmer for **20 minutes** until liquid is absorbed.
- Fluff with a fork and let rest for 5 minutes.

**3. Prepare Avocado-Mango Salsa (Low-FODMAP Version)**
- In a medium bowl, toss together:
  - **1 large mango**, diced
  - **3/4 cup yellow bell pepper**, chopped
  - **1/4 cup cilantro**, chopped
  - **1/4 cup green onion tops**, sliced (green parts only)
  - **1 large avocado**, diced
  - **1 tbsp lime juice**
  - **1 tbsp garlic-infused oil**
  - **1 tbsp coconut water**
- Season with salt and pepper.

### Cook: 10 minutes

**4. Grill the Salmon**
- Preheat grill to medium-high. Brush grates with oil.
- Grill salmon for **3-4 minutes per side** until just cooked through and opaque.
- Handle carefully to keep fillets intact.
