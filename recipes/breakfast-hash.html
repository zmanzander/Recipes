🥔 Yukon Gold Turkey & Parsnip Hash

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Time: 45 mins
Storage: 5 days in fridge
EF / No Sweet Potato

Ingredients (Shared Batch: 10 portions)

  • 3 lbs Ground Turkey (93% lean)
  • 2 lbs Yukon Gold Potatoes (1/2-inch dice)
  • 1 lb Parsnips (1/2-inch dice)
  • 3 medium Carrots (1/4-inch dice)
  • 2 medium Yellow Bell Peppers (diced)
  • 1 bunch Green Onion Tops (sliced)
  • 4 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add the 2 lbs diced potatoes and 1 lb diced parsnips. Boil for 5-7 mins until par-cooked (fork-tender but not mushy). Drain and set aside.
Heat 2 tbsp garlic-infused oil in a very large skillet or wok over Medium-High Heat.
Add the 3 lbs ground turkey. Cook, breaking up with a spatula, until browned and cooked through.
Instruction Update: Once browned, add the 1 tbsp smoked paprika, 1 tsp cumin, and salt/pepper. Stir well to coat the meat before it dries out. Remove turkey and set aside.
Wipe skillet if needed, then add the remaining 2 tbsp garlic oil. Add the 3 diced carrots and the par-cooked potatoes/parsnips.
Sauté over Medium Heat for 10-12 mins, flipping occasionally, until everything is golden and crispy.
Add the 2 diced yellow peppers and sliced green onion tops. Sauté for 3-5 mins until softened.
Return the seasoned turkey to the pan. Toss everything together and heat through for 2 mins.

Portioning Guide (Cooked)
