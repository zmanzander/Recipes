Potato, Parsnip & Turkey Breakfast Hash

⏱️ Prep: 15 mins | 🍳 Cook: 30 mins | 📦 Makes: 10 Portions

Ingredients

  • Ground Turkey: 96.5 oz raw total (Zander 5x 12oz, Hanna 5x 7.3oz)
  • Potatoes: 4 large Regular Potatoes (Yukon Gold or Russet), peeled and cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • Parsnips: 3 large Parsnips, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch cubes
  • Carrots: 2 cups Shredded Carrots
  • Yellow Bell Peppers: 3 Yellow Bell Peppers, cut into 1/4-inch dice
  • Spinach: 4 cups Fresh Spinach
  • Green Onions: 1 cup Green Onion tops (green part only), finely chopped
  • Oil: 4 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil (divided)
  • Spices: 1 tbsp Turmeric, 1 tbsp Cumin, and Sea Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Sauté Roots: Heat 2 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil in a very large skillet or pot over medium-high heat. Add the 4 large cubed potatoes and 3 large cubed parsnips. Cook for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the roots are softened and have developed a light golden-brown crust.
  2. Add Protein: To the same skillet, add the remaining 2 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil, 96.5 oz ground turkey, and 2 cups shredded carrots. Season with 1 tbsp turmeric, 1 tbsp cumin, and sea salt.
  3. Brown Turkey: Continue cooking over medium-high heat for 8-10 minutes. Use a spatula to break the ground turkey into small crumbles as it browns, until no pink remains.
  4. Add Peppers: Stir in the 3 diced yellow bell peppers and 1 cup chopped green onion tops. Cook over medium heat for 5 minutes, allowing the peppers to soften slightly while retaining their color.
  5. Wilt Spinach: Add 4 cups fresh spinach to the skillet. Gently fold the spinach into the hash for 1-2 minutes until just wilted.
  6. Portion: Remove from heat and allow to cool slightly before dividing into 10 airtight containers for the week.

Portioning (Cooked Volume)

  • Zander 5 Portions: Approx. 2.5 cups (or 9 oz cooked weight) per meal.
  • Hanna 5 Portions: Approx. 1.5 cups (or 5.5 oz cooked weight) per meal.

Medical Compliance

✅ Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Egg-free, Soy-free.
🚫 No garlic/onion pieces (infused oil and tops only).
🚫 No sweet potatoes (using compliant white potatoes/parsnips).
