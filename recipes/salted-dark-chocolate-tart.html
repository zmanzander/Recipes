Salted Dark Chocolate Tart

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Time: 27 mins
Style: Dessert (Dinner Party / Treat)

Ingredients (One 9-inch Tart)

For the Crust:

  • 2 cups Almond Flour
  • 1/4 cup Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • 1/4 cup Melted Coconut Oil (or butter substitute)
  • 3 tbsp Maple Syrup
  • 1/2 tsp Fine Sea Salt

For the Chocolate Filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups Full-fat Coconut Milk (warmed)
  • 1 lb (16 oz) Dark Chocolate (70%+, roughly-chopped)
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/4 tsp Fine Sea Salt
  • Topping: Flaky Sea Salt (Maldon)

Step-by-Step Instructions

Preheat Oven to 350°F. Mix all crust ingredients until evenly combined. Press into the bottom of a 9-inch tart pan.
Bake for 12 Minutes until dry and firm. Remove and cool on wire rack.
Prepare Filling: Heat 1.5 cups coconut milk until simmering. Pour over 1 lb chopped chocolate. Let sit for 30s.
Stir until Smooth: Slowly stir until chocolate is melted. Mix in vanilla and salt.
Final Chill: Pour filling into crust. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours until firm.

Zander-Optimized Portion Guide

Standard Serving (1/12th Tart): ~7.5g Protein | ~25g Saturated Fat.

Note: High in saturated fat due to coconut milk and chocolate. One slice exceeds daily 20g target (Sat Fat: 25g).
