Grilled Lime Salmon with Avocado-Mango Salsa

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Time: 55 mins
Style: Grill (Guest Dinner)

Ingredients (For 4 Portions)

For the Salmon:

  • 2.2 lbs Skinless Salmon Fillets
  • 3 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil (for marinade)
  • 2 tsp Fresh Lime Zest
  • 3 tbsp Fresh Lime Juice
  • To taste Salt & Black Pepper

For the Coconut Rice:

  • 1.5 cups Jasmine Rice (rinsed)
  • 1.5 cups Coconut Water
  • 1.25 cups Full-fat Coconut Milk
  • 1/2 tsp Salt

For the Salsa (Low-FODMAP Version):

  • 1 large Ripe Mango (diced)
  • 3/4 cup Yellow Bell Pepper (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup Fresh Cilantro (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup Green Onion tops (green parts only)
  • 1 large Avocado (diced)
  • 1 tbsp Lime Juice
  • 1 tbsp Garlic-infused Oil
  • 1 tbsp Coconut Water

Step-by-Step Instructions

Marinate: In a baking dish, whisk together lime zest, lime juice, garlic-infused oil, salt and pepper. Place 2.2 lbs salmon in the dish. Marinate for 30-60 mins in the fridge, flipping once.
Cook Rice: In a saucepan, bring coconut water, coconut milk, rice, and salt to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 mins. Fluff and rest 5 mins.
Make Salsa: Toss diced mango, yellow pepper, avocado, cilantro, and green onion tops with lime juice, oil, and coconut water. Season with salt and pepper.
Grill Salmon: Preheat grill to Medium-High Heat. Grill salmon for 3-4 mins per side until opaque and tender.
Serve: Plate the salmon over coconut rice and top with 1/4 cup salsa per person. Serve warm.

Zander-Optimized Portion Guide (4 Portions)

Alexander (1 meal): 9 oz cooked salmon + 1.5 cups rice + 1/4 cup salsa.

Hanna & Guests (3 meals): 5.5 oz cooked salmon + 1 cup rice + 1/4 cup salsa per person.

FODMAP Limit: Do not exceed 1/4 cup salsa per serving due to mango/avocado content.
