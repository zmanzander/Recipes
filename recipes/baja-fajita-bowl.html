Baja Fajita Bowl

Gluten-FreeDairy-FreeEgg-FreeSoy-FreeLow-FODMAP
Week 6 • Wednesday Fresh Cook
Cuisine: Mexican-Inspired Style: Quick Fresh Cook Prep: 15 min Cook: 20 min Total: 35 min

Ingredients

  • Beef: 1.5 lbs Ground Beef (90/10 lean)
  • Vegetables: 3 Yellow Bell Peppers (sliced), 1 bunch Cilantro (chopped)
  • Baja Sauce (Custom): 3 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil, 2 tbsp Lime Juice, 1 tbsp GF/SF Tamari, 1 tsp Cumin, 1/2 tsp Oregano
  • Base: 1.5 cups dry Jasmine Rice

Instructions

  1. Cook Rice: Rinse 1.5 cups jasmine rice. Add to pot with 2.25 cups water. Bring to boil, simmer covered 18 mins, rest 5 mins.
  2. Prep Sauce: Whisk 3 tbsp garlic oil, 2 tbsp lime juice, 1 tbsp tamari, 1 tsp cumin, and 1/2 tsp oregano.
  3. Sauté Peppers: Heat 1 tbsp garlic oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Add 3 sliced yellow peppers. Cook 5-7 mins until tender-crisp. Remove from pan.
  4. Brown Beef: In the same pan, add 1.5 lbs ground beef. Break apart and cook 6-8 mins until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Combine: Add peppers and Baja sauce to the beef. Toss to coat and simmer for 2 mins. Stir in fresh cilantro.

Portion Guide (Cooked)

Alexander: 9 oz beef/pepper mix over 1.5 cups rice.

Hanna: 6 oz beef/pepper mix over 1 cup rice.

Medical Compliance

✅ Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Soy-free, Egg-free.
🚫 No onion/garlic pieces (infused oil only).
🟨 Yellow bell peppers only.
