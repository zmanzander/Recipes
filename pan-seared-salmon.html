Pan-Seared Salmon with Bok Choy

Prep time: 10 mins | Cook time: 15 mins

Ingredients (3 Portions)

  • Salmon Fillets: 31.3oz raw total (Zander 2x 12oz, Hanna 1x 7.3oz)
  • 2 heads Bok Choy (leafy green parts only)
  • 1 tbsp Fresh Ginger (grated)
  • 2 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • 1 Lemon (juiced)
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Dill (chopped)
  • 1.5 cups White Rice (dry)
  • Sea salt

Instructions

  1. Cook white rice according to package instructions.
  2. Season salmon with salt, dill, and lemon juice.
  3. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Sear salmon for 4-5 mins per side until cooked through.
  4. In a separate pan, heat 1 tbsp oil. Sauté ginger for 1 min. Add bok choy leaves and sauté until wilted (3-4 mins).
  5. Serve salmon over rice with a side of bok choy.

Portioning

  • Monday Dinner: Zander (12oz raw)
  • Tuesday Lunch: Zander (12oz raw), Hanna (7.3oz raw)

Medical Compliance

Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Egg-free, Soy-free. No garlic/onion pieces. Ginger and dill used for flavor.
