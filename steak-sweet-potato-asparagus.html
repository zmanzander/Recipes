Pan-Seared Steak with Roasted Sweet Potato

Week 3 • Weekend Fresh Cook (Sat-Sun)
Cuisine: American Style: Fresh Cook Prep: 10 min Cook: 20 min Total: 30 min Makes: 8 portions total

What You're Making

  • Saturday: Lunch + Dinner (Zander + Hanna)
  • Sunday: Lunch + Dinner (Zander + Hanna)

Ingredients

Proteins

  • 2 lbs Steak (Sirloin or Ribeye)

Sides

  • 4 large Sweet Potatoes
  • 2 bunches Asparagus

Seasoning

  • Olive oil
  • Sea salt & Black pepper
  • Rosemary (fresh or dried)

Instructions

Step 1: Roast Sweet Potatoes & Asparagus (20 min)

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Cube 4 large sweet potatoes and toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread on a sheet pan.
  3. Roast for 15 minutes.
  4. Trim 2 bunches asparagus. Add to the sheet pan (or a separate pan) and toss with olive oil/salt. Roast for an additional 10-12 minutes until everything is tender.

Step 2: Sear Steak (10 min)

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat with a high-heat oil (avocado or vegetable).
  2. Season 2 lbs steak generously with sea salt, black pepper, and rosemary.
  3. Sear steak for 4-5 minutes per side (for medium-rare/medium, depending on thickness).
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes before slicing against the grain.

Step 3: Portion & Enjoy

  1. Zander's plates: 10 oz steak + 1.5 sweet potato + 1 bunch asparagus.
  2. Hanna's plates: 6 oz steak + 1 small sweet potato + 1/2 bunch asparagus.

Tips

Resting: Resting the steak is crucial for juicy meat. Don't skip it!

Variations: Add a dollop of butter and minced garlic to the pan during the last minute of searing for extra flavor.
