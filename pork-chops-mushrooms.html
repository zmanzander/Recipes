Pork Chops with Oyster Mushrooms

Prep time: 15 mins | Cook time: 25 mins

Ingredients (3 Portions)

  • Pork Chops: 26.6oz raw total (Zander 1x 12oz, Hanna 2x 7.3oz)
  • 8oz Oyster Mushrooms (sliced)
  • 2 medium Sweet Potatoes (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Rosemary & Thyme (chopped)
  • 3 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • Sea salt

Instructions

  1. Toss sweet potato cubes with 1 tbsp oil and salt. Roast at 400°F for 25 mins.
  2. Season pork chops with salt and fresh herbs.
  3. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a skillet. Sear pork chops for 5-7 mins per side until internal temp is 145°F. Remove and rest.
  4. In the same skillet, add 1 tbsp oil and oyster mushrooms. Sauté until golden (5-7 mins).
  5. Serve pork chops topped with mushrooms and roasted sweet potatoes on the side.

Portioning

  • Wednesday Dinner: Zander (12oz raw), Hanna (7.3oz raw)
  • Thursday Lunch: Hanna (7.3oz raw)

Medical Compliance

Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Egg-free, Soy-free. Safe mushrooms (Oyster) and potatoes.
