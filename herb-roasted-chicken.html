Herb-Roasted Chicken & Vegetables

Prep time: 20 mins | Cook time: 1 hour 15 mins

Ingredients (4 Portions)

  • 1 Whole Chicken (approx. 4.5 - 5 lbs to yield ~40oz raw meat)
  • 4 large Carrots, chopped
  • 3 Zucchini (peeled and sliced)
  • 2 Red Bell Peppers (sliced)
  • 4 tbsp Garlic-infused Olive Oil
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Rosemary (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Thyme (chopped)
  • 1 Lemon (halved)
  • Sea salt and black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Pat chicken dry. Rub with 2 tbsp garlic-infused oil, rosemary, thyme, salt, and pepper. Place lemon halves inside cavity.
  3. Toss carrots, peeled zucchini, and bell peppers with remaining 2 tbsp oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Place chicken in a roasting pan. Surround with vegetables.
  5. Roast for 1 hour 15 mins or until internal temperature reaches 165°F.
  6. Let rest for 10 minutes before carving.

Portioning

  • Sunday Dinner: Zander (12oz raw weight equivalent), Hanna (7.3oz raw weight equivalent)
  • Monday Lunch: Zander (12oz raw weight equivalent), Hanna (7.3oz raw weight equivalent)

Medical Compliance

Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Egg-free, Soy-free. No garlic/onion pieces. Used garlic-infused oil.
